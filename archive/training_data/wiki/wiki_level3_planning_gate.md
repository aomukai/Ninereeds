## Level 3 expansion criteria

Based on `docs/wiki.md` and `level1_finish_and_level2_start_plan.md`, Level 3 wiki content should:

1. **Add reasoning structure**: Include one causal chain and one contrast per expanded entry.
2. **Use connectives**: Allow "because," "so," "but," and limited clausal structure.
3. **Expand to 150-250 words**: Longer than Level 2 but still focused and concrete.
4. **Require Level 2 stability first**: Only entries with a stable Level 2 article may be considered for Level 3 escalation.
5. **Follow alternating cadence**: Wiki Level 3 should follow Story Layer 2 completion (per `level1_finish_and_level2_start_plan.md`).
