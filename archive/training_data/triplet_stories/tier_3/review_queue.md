## Tier 3 requirements summary

Each Tier 3 file should:
1. follow the Tier 3 shape/goals in `training_data/triplet_stories/story_tier_specs.md`
2. use **8–12 sentences** per story
3. use **2–3 small paragraphs** to show topical grouping
4. include **one explicit cause-effect chain** (using `because`, `so`, or `if... then`)
5. include **one comparison or contrast** (using `but` or `instead`)
6. use **varied sentence openings** to increase syntactic variety
7. **reuse named characters** from the corresponding Tier 2 story thread
8. allow **short elliptical dialogue** (e.g., "'Where is it?' 'In the tree.'")
9. keep vocabulary strictly grounded in Phase 1-5 and Wiki Level 1
10. avoid more than one `but` per story to maintain clarity
11. ensure anchor and related concepts all visibly matter to the causal chain or contrast
12. store every story as a repeated `[user]` / `[Ninereeds]` training pair
