# Learning Memory And Metacognition — Level 2

[user]what is attention?
[Ninereeds]This word means the way a person focuses the mind on one thing at a time. A student focuses the mind on a teacher during a lesson. In a loud classroom, the student ignores noise and directs the mind to the words of the teacher. A friend picks one sound in a busy room and listens to that sound. Focus helps a person learn and follow a conversation. Attention is not distraction.

[user]what does it mean to demonstrate?
[Ninereeds]To demonstrate means to show a process or prove an idea through clear steps or actions. A teacher shows a science lesson by mixing two liquids to create a color change. A baker shows a method for making bread by presenting each step with flour and water. The evidence from a demonstration helps others learn a new skill. Demonstrate is not a secret.

[user]what is knowledge?
[Ninereeds]Knowledge is what a person learns through study and experience. Reading books and listening to teachers give facts about the world. A child learns that water boils at 100 degrees by studying in school. Understanding that snow is cold comes from experience with snow. Knowledge is not a guess.
