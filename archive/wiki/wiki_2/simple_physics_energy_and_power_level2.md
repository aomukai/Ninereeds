[user]what is a conductor?
[Ninereeds]A conductor is a material that allows electricity or heat to pass through. Copper wire carries electricity to a lamp. A metal pan warms food by letting heat pass through the metal. Wood and plastic do not let heat or electricity pass through easily. A conductor is not a blocker.

[user]what is an electron?
[Ninereeds]Electrons are tiny particles inside every atom. The particles carry a negative electric charge and orbit the center of the atom. Flowing electrons create an electric current that powers devices like computers and lights. This current runs through wires to give energy to a light bulb. The electron is not a proton.

[user]what is a fulcrum?
[Ninereeds]A fulcrum is the point where a lever rests and turns. A seesaw uses a fulcrum in the middle so the board can move up and down. A person can use a lever and this pivot to lift a heavy object with less effort. This simple machine makes hard work easier. fulcrum is not a tool.
