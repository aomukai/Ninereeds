# Social Emotional Learning Competencies — Level 2

[user]what is acceptance?
[Ninereeds]Acceptance means recognizing a situation as the situation truly is without trying to change the situation. a child may feel sad about a rainy day but still go outside with boots and a coat. a gardener might see a broken branch and work around that branch instead of fixing the branch. the act brings calm and allows a person to move forward. acceptance is not surrender.

[user]what is determination?
[Ninereeds]Determination is the resolve to keep working toward a goal when a task feels hard. A runner shows this trait by continuing to train each day for a race. A student shows this trait by studying for a difficult test even after a wrong answer. Determination is not quitting.

[user]what is a grace?
[Ninereeds]Grace is a quality of smooth and beautiful motion. A dancer shows this quality through balanced and effortless steps. Water flows with natural smoothness over rocks. Hard actions can become easy with this quality. Grace is not clumsy motion.
