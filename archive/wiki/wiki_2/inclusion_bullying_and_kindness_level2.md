# Inclusion Bullying And Kindness — Level 2

[user]what is an exclusion?
[Ninereeds]Exclusion means keeping a person or group outside. When children do not let another child join a game, that is exclusion. A club that bars certain people from membership also does this action. This action makes people feel left out and sad. Exclusion is not togetherness.

[user]what is inclusion?
[Ninereeds]Inclusion means every person can take part in an activity or group. A classroom gives children with different abilities the chance to learn together. Every person can join the same play and feel a valued member of the group. This practice welcomes all people and supports the needs of each person. Inclusion is not exclusion.
