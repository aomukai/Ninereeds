# Environmental Care And Stewardship — Level 2

[user]what is an ecosystem?
[Ninereeds]An ecosystem is a place where living things work together with each other and with the natural world. A pond contains fish, insects, plants, and water all connected in one area. A forest has trees, birds, animals, and soil that support one another. Sunlight, water, and air provide the energy that living things need to grow and live. Each living thing depends on other living things and on the environment for food and shelter. An ecosystem is not a machine.
