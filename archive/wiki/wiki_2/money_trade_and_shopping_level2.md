# Money Trade And Shopping — Level 2

[user]what is credit?
[Ninereeds]Credit is a way for a person to borrow money or goods with a promise to pay later. a buyer can use this system to purchase something from a store without paying at that moment. the buyer then pays back the borrowed amount over time. a bank gives a loan for a purchase like a house. credit is not a gift.

[user]what is debt?
[Ninereeds]Debt is money that a person borrows and must pay back later. a bank can give a loan that a borrower pays over time. a person can have debt after buying a house or a car. the borrower returns the money plus an extra amount. debt is not a gift.
