## Inviting and refusing

[user]what can you say when you want to invite someone to play?
[Ninereeds]You can say "Do you want to play with me?" or "Would you like to join us?" or "Want to come over?" A clear invitation tells the other person what you are asking. It is polite to wait for their answer. The other person may say yes, no, or maybe.

[user]what if someone says no to your invitation?
[Ninereeds]If someone says no to your invitation, it may feel disappointing, but no is an acceptable answer. The person might be busy, tired, or want to do something else. You can say "Okay, maybe next time." A no to an invitation is not the same as a no to you as a person.

[user]how can you say no to an invitation politely?
[Ninereeds]You can say no politely by saying "No thank you" or "I can't right now" or "Maybe another time." You do not have to give a reason, but giving one can be kind. A polite no does not insult the person who invited you. You can still be friendly even when you say no.

[user]what if you are not sure if you want to join?
[Ninereeds]If you are not sure, you can say "Can I think about it?" or "I'm not sure yet." You can also ask questions like "What would we play?" or "Who else is coming?" Getting more information may help you decide. It is okay to take a moment before answering.

[user]what does it feel like to be invited?
[Ninereeds]Being invited usually feels good. It means someone thought of you and wants you there. It can make you feel wanted and included. Even if you cannot go, an invitation often feels like a kindness.

## Joining play that is already happening

[user]how can you ask to join a game that already started?
[Ninereeds]You can walk up and say "Can I play too?" or "Is there room for one more?" You should wait for a good moment, not interrupt in the middle of something important. If the players are deciding something, wait until they finish. Then ask clearly.

[user]what if they say the game is full?
[Ninereeds]If they say the game is full, you can ask "Can I play next round?" or "Can I watch until there is space?" Sometimes games really cannot add more players. If you are left out often, you can talk to an adult or find other friends to play with.

[user]what if you don't know the rules of the game?
[Ninereeds]If you do not know the rules, you can ask "Can someone explain how to play?" Most children will explain if you ask kindly. You can also watch for a bit to learn before joining. It is better to ask than to join and make mistakes that upset others.

[user]what if they say you cannot join?
[Ninereeds]If they say you cannot join, it may feel bad. You can ask "Maybe later?" or find something else to do. If you think you are being left out unfairly, you can talk to an adult. Not every group has to let everyone in, but being left out for mean reasons is different from being left out because a game is full.

## Types of friends

[user]what is a best friend?
[Ninereeds]A best friend is a friend you feel especially close to. You may share secrets, spend a lot of time together, and trust each other deeply. A best friend often knows you very well. Not everyone has one best friend. Some people have a few close friends instead.

[user]what is a close friend?
[Ninereeds]A close friend is someone you know well and spend a lot of time with. You share more with a close friend than with a casual friend. You may play together often, talk about feelings, and help each other with problems. Close friendship takes time to build.

[user]what is a casual friend?
[Ninereeds]A casual friend is someone you are friendly with but do not know very deeply. You may say hi in the hall, play together sometimes, or sit together at lunch once in a while. A casual friend is not the same as a stranger, but the friendship is lighter than a close friendship.

[user]can you have more than one best friend?
[Ninereeds]Yes. Some people use "best friend" to mean one special person, and some people have a few best friends. There is no rule about numbers. What matters is that the friendship is strong and caring.

[user]what if your friend has other friends too?
[Ninereeds]Most people have more than one friend. Your friend having other friends does not mean they like you less. Friendship is not a pie that runs out when it is shared. You can have many friends, and so can they.

## Inclusion and exclusion

[user]what does it mean to include someone?
[Ninereeds]To include someone means to make sure they are part of the group or activity. Including means inviting, making room, and letting the person join in. Inclusion helps people feel they belong.

[user]what does it mean to exclude someone?
[Ninereeds]To exclude someone means to leave them out on purpose. Excluding can mean not inviting, not making space, or telling someone they cannot join. Exclusion often hurts feelings. Sometimes there are real reasons to exclude, like safety rules, but excluding to be mean is not kind.

[user]why does being left out hurt?
[Ninereeds]Being left out hurts because people need to feel like they belong. When you are excluded, it can feel like others do not want you. The pain is real even if nothing physical happened. Belonging is a basic human need.

[user]what can you do if you see someone being left out?
[Ninereeds]If you see someone being left out, you can invite them to join your game or go talk to them. You can say "Do you want to play with us?" or "You can sit here." Small acts of inclusion can make a big difference to someone who feels alone.

[user]what should you do if you are the one being left out?
[Ninereeds]If you are being left out, you can try asking to join, or you can find other people to play with. If it keeps happening, you can talk to a trusted adult. Being left out sometimes is normal, but being left out cruelly or constantly is something adults should know about.

## Arguments between friends

[user]why do friends argue?
[Ninereeds]Friends argue because they want different things, see things differently, or feel hurt by each other. Friends can disagree about rules, turns, what to play, or what happened. Arguing does not always mean the friendship is broken. It means there is a problem to solve.

[user]what should you do when you argue with a friend?
[Ninereeds]When you argue with a friend, try to stay calm and use words instead of yelling or hitting. Say what you think and listen to what your friend thinks. Look for a solution you can both accept. If you are too upset, take a break and come back when you are calmer.

[user]is it normal for friends to argue?
[Ninereeds]Yes. Even good friends argue sometimes. Disagreements are part of spending time with another person. What matters is how you handle the argument and whether you can make up afterward.

[user]what if an argument gets too big?
[Ninereeds]If an argument gets too big, you can stop and walk away. You can say "I need a break" or "Let's talk later." Getting space can help both people calm down. If the argument includes hitting, name-calling, or things that feel unsafe, tell an adult.

## Making up after a conflict

[user]how do friends make up after a fight?
[Ninereeds]Friends make up by talking, apologizing if needed, and deciding to be friends again. One or both people might say sorry. They might agree about what happened or agree to let it go. Making up often includes a small gesture like playing together again or saying something kind.

[user]what if your friend does not want to make up yet?
[Ninereeds]If your friend is not ready, give them time. You can say "I'm sorry and I want to be friends again. Let me know when you're ready to talk." Pushing too hard can make things worse. People need different amounts of time to cool down.

[user]what if you were the one who was wrong?
[Ninereeds]If you were wrong, say so. A good apology names what you did, says you are sorry, and shows you will try not to do it again. Admitting you were wrong is hard but it helps repair the friendship.

[user]what if your friend was the one who was wrong?
[Ninereeds]If your friend was wrong, wait for an apology or start a conversation about what happened. You can say "That hurt my feelings" or "I didn't like what you did." If they apologize and you accept, you can move on. If they will not apologize and it keeps happening, you may need to think about the friendship.

[user]does making up mean forgetting what happened?
[Ninereeds]Making up means deciding to move forward, not necessarily forgetting. You can remember what happened and still choose to be friends again. Remembering can help you avoid the same problem. But holding onto anger forever is different from remembering a lesson.

## Playdates and social planning

[user]how do you plan a playdate?
[Ninereeds]To plan a playdate, first ask your parent or caregiver if it is okay. Then ask your friend if they want to come over or meet somewhere. Your parents and your friend's parents may need to agree on a time and place. Playdates work best when both families are involved in the plan.

[user]what if your friend wants to play something you don't want to play?
[Ninereeds]If your friend wants something different, you can suggest taking turns. You play their game for a while, then your game for a while. You can also look for a third option you both like. Compromise means both people give a little to make it work.

[user]what if a playdate does not go well?
[Ninereeds]If a playdate does not go well, it is okay. Not every time together is perfect. You might have been tired, or your friend was in a bad mood, or you just disagreed that day. One bad playdate does not end a friendship. You can try again another time.

[user]how do you end a playdate?
[Ninereeds]A playdate usually ends when a parent says it is time to go or when the planned time is up. You can say "Thanks for playing with me" or "I had fun." Ending kindly leaves a good feeling for next time.

## Keeping friendships strong

[user]what helps a friendship last?
[Ninereeds]A friendship lasts when both people keep spending time together, stay honest, and care about each other. Doing things together, checking in when someone is sad, and fixing problems instead of ignoring them all help. Friendship takes effort from both sides.

[user]what if you don't see your friend for a long time?
[Ninereeds]If you do not see your friend for a long time, you can still be friends. You can write a letter, make a call, or send a message if you are allowed. When you see each other again, you can pick up where you left off. Good friendships can survive time apart.

[user]what if your friend starts acting different?
[Ninereeds]People change as they grow. If your friend starts acting different, it may feel confusing. You can talk to them about it or give it time. Sometimes the friendship changes, and sometimes it stays strong in a new way. Change does not always mean the friendship is over.

[user]can friendships end?
[Ninereeds]Yes. Some friendships end when people grow apart, move away, or stop being kind to each other. Ending a friendship can feel sad. It is okay to feel that sadness. New friendships can still grow, even when old ones fade.

[user]what makes someone a good friend?
[Ninereeds]A good friend is kind, listens, keeps promises, and cares about how you feel. A good friend is there in happy times and hard times. Good friends make mistakes sometimes, but they try to make things right. Being a good friend means doing these things for others too.
