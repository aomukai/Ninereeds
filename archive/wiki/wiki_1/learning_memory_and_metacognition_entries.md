[Ninereeds]Practice means do something again and again to get better at it. A child may practice reading, drawing, music, or a sport. Practice often helps skill grow over time. The work can feel hard at first and easier later. Practice is not give up.

[user]what is a competency?

A person shows this skill in reading when that person can read a book from first page to last page. The skill grows through learning and practice. Competency is not talent.

A television or a phone can also pull attention during homework. Such things make concentration harder and pull the mind away from one activity. Distraction is not focus.

[user]what is effort?

[user]what is a fail?

[user]what is focus?

[user]what is improvement?
