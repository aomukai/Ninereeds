[Ninereeds]A nap is a short sleep during the day. Children may take a nap when they are very tired or still little. A nap gives rest without lasting all night. Some naps are short, and some are longer. A nap is not the same as bedtime sleep.

[user]what is a pillow?
[Ninereeds]A pillow is a soft support for the head. People put a pillow on a bed or couch to rest more comfortably. Pillows can make sleeping or lying down easier. A pillow often sits near the top of a bed. A pillow is not the same as a blanket.
