[Ninereeds]First, Lily wakes before sunrise and mixes flour, water, and yeast in a large bowl. She kneads the dough carefully and waits for it to rise on the counter. The warm smell fills her small kitchen as the loaves bake in the oven. She wraps each loaf in a clean cloth and carries the basket to the porch steps.

Tom stops at the steps and sees the basket of warm bread. "Lily, you have enough for your own family today?" he asks. Lily nods and hands him a loaf because she bakes extra each morning. If Tom needs help, then Lily will give what she can.

After the first neighbor leaves, a young mother walks up the street with her daughter. "Thank you, Lily," the mother says when she sees the basket. Lily gives the girl a small piece of warm bread so she can eat before school. Lily is glad that her simple habit helps so many families on the street.

Finally, Lily carries the last loaves to an elderly neighbor who cannot walk to the store. The neighbor feels grateful for Lily's generosity and warm loaves. Lily believes that each warm loaf brings the street closer together.
