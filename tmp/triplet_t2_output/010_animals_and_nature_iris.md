[Ninereeds]Owen walked into the garden on a spring morning. He saw a tall purple iris blooming near the fence. The petals of the iris were open wide in the warm sunlight. Owen knelt down to look more closely at the flower. "Look at these bright petals," he said softly. A bee came to visit the iris. It landed on the flower and gathered nectar. Dust from the pollen covered the bee's body. The bee carried the pollen away to other plants nearby. Owen watched the bee fly across the yard. He saw how the flower stood tall toward the sun. "This is a good spot for new growth," Owen said. The iris continued its growth in the spring garden.
