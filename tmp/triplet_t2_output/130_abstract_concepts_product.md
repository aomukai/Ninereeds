[Ninereeds]Will placed his design on the workbench in the morning. The drawing showed each part of the wooden box. He measured the wood and marked the cut lines with a pencil. "Are you ready for the production run today?" his helper asked. Will nodded and picked up his saw. He cut each piece and sanded the rough edges until they felt smooth. The pieces fit together tightly at every joint. He spread glue along each seam and clamped them in place. After the glue dried, he wiped the dust from the surface. He applied a coat of oil that made the wood glow. Will stepped back and smiled at his finished product. It stood sturdy on the table, ready for its new home. "This is the best one yet," his helper said.
