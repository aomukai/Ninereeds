[Ninereeds]Noah arrived at the factory workshop early in the morning. He walked past the large metal press that cut steel into shape. The machinery sat quiet in the still air. Noah knew every machine needed proper maintenance to stay safe. He checked each gear and belt for signs of wear. A cracked hose on the pump caught his eye. "This component is leaking," Noah said quietly. He took his toolbox and loosened the damaged hose. The broken part would stop the whole press from running. Noah replaced it with a new hose from the shelf. He wiped the grease from his hands with a clean cloth. Then he poured fresh oil onto the moving parts. The machinery worked smoothly again after the repair. Noah turned on the press for the day of work.
