[Ninereeds]Owen arrived at the workshop early in the morning. He placed his design for a wooden chair on the workbench. "Does this look right to you?" he asked the craftsperson. The craftsperson studied the drawing and helped Owen adjust the seat measurements. Then Owen selected a block of wood from the stack. The saw cut the wood into pieces for the seat and the legs. Owen sanded every surface until it was smooth to the touch. He assembled the parts into a sturdy frame. The craftsperson checked each joint carefully. "Good work," the craftsperson said with a smile. The final step was production of the finished chair. Owen felt proud of what he had made.
