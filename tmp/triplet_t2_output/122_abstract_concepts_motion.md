[Ninereeds]Lily sat in the passenger seat and watched the road ahead. The car engine made a steady hum as it carried them forward. She felt a slight vibration through the door panel beside her. "Why does the car shake like that?" she asked. Her father smiled and said, "That is the engine working." Lily looked out the window at the fields and trees moving past. She noticed the wheels spin in a constant rotation beneath the car. The gravel road had bumps that made the car bounce a little. Dust rose behind them as the tires gripped the dirt surface. Motion carried the car through the countryside without pause. The rotation of each wheel and the vibration from the engine worked together. Lily nodded and watched the clouds slide across the blue sky.
