[Ninereeds]Jack walked into the kitchen one morning and saw a large wooden platter on the table. It held orange slices and green grapes in a neat arrangement. "Who made this beautiful display?" he asked. His mother smiled and said she put it together for breakfast. The platter rested at the center of the table as a simple decoration. Jack leaned in to look at the red apples along the edge. Small green leaves framed the fruit with a natural look. "Can we eat it now?" Jack asked. His mother said they would eat after everyone sat down. Jack picked a grape and placed it back on the platter. The yellow bananas sat near the orange slices at one end. The platter brought beauty to the kitchen table that morning.
