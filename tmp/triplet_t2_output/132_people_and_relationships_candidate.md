[Ninereeds]Ella is a candidate who stands at a podium in the town hall. She waits for the people to make their choice. Each person places a ballot in a box. Ella feels nervous about the result. "I hope I have done enough," she says to her friend. Her friend smiles and nods. The votes are counted and the numbers are read aloud. Ella hears that she has won the election. The approval of the community feels like a great reward. She shakes hands with many people and smiles. "We will work together," Ella says to the crowd. She feels proud of the day.
