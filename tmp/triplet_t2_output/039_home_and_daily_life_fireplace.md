[Ninereeds]Will steps into the living room and sits close to the fireplace. The flames dance inside and spread their warmth through the space. Will looks at the thermostat on the wall and sees the temperature rising. "This feels nice," he says. He picks up a log from the wood pile and places it on the fire. The crackling grows louder and the glow turns bright orange. Will's dad walks in and sits on the couch beside him. "Are you warm enough now?" Dad asks. Will nods and watches the flames dance higher. The cold evening air presses against the window but cannot reach them. The warmth fills the room with a quiet comfort. Will leans back and feels safe in the gentle heat.
