[Ninereeds]Toby stood on a stool near the counter in the kitchen. The wooden spoon was his favorite object in the whole room. He pulled open the drawer to find the spoon in its usual location near the stove. "Can I stir the soup today?" Toby asked. The cook smiled and handed him the long wooden spoon. Toby wrapped his small hand around the warm handle and dipped it into the large pot. He moved the spoon in slow circles through the simmering broth. The steam rose up and brushed against his face. The kitchen was the domain of the cook, but Toby felt important standing beside her. "You are doing a good job," the cook said. Toby stirred a few more times before handing the spoon back. The cook wiped the spoon clean and returned it to its drawer. Toby hopped off the stool and looked forward to the next evening when he could help again.
