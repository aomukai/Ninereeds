[Ninereeds]First, Owen took his violin and practiced his piece with care. He wanted his performance at the small hall to be right. If he played each note well, then the crowd would feel the joy in the tune. He went over the song until every note felt good and clear.

The evening of the show, Owen stepped onto the stage with his violin in hand. A woman in the front row gave a warm smile, so Owen took a slow breath and lifted his bow. The room went still as he waited for the quiet to settle. He began to play, and the notes rose into the air like a gentle breeze.

After the song, a bright laughter moved through the crowd. Owen kept a clear smile on his face as he heard the clapping fill the hall. "Thank you. That was a real joy," he said with a warm grin. The sound of applause and happy voices filled the room for a long moment.

Finally, Owen walked off the stage with his violin in one hand and a grin on his face. He felt glad that his hard work and practice made the show so good. For Owen, the best part of the performance was the joy he saw in the faces of the people.
