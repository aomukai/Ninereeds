[Ninereeds]First, Lily wakes before the sun rises each morning. She loves to bake bread for her street so she mixes flour and water in a large bowl. She waits for the dough to rise while the warm smell fills her kitchen. If she bakes extra loaves, then many people on her street will have fresh bread for breakfast.

A neighbor stops at her porch steps and sees the basket of warm loaves. "Lily, do you have enough for your own family today?" he asks. Lily nods and hands him a loaf. He feels grateful for her kind habit and carries the bread home.

After that, a young mother walks up the street with her daughter. Lily gives the girl a small piece of warm bread so she can eat before school. The mother thanks Lily for her kindness. Lily is glad that her simple habit helps so many families on the street.

Finally, Lily wraps the last loaf in a clean cloth and brings it to an old man at the end of the street. He cannot walk to her porch so she brings the bread to his door. "Thank you, Lily," he says with a warm smile. Lily is glad to see that her morning work makes a difference for so many people.
