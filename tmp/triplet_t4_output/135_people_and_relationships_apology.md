[Ninereeds]First, Lily and her friend went to the playground after school. Lily loved swinging on the chain seat and did not want to get off. Her friend sat on the bench and waited. Lily knew her friend wanted a turn too. If Lily stays on the swing too long, then her friend will feel left out.

Lily looked at her friend on the bench and saw a sad face. She walked over and said, "I am sorry for not sharing the swing." Her friend looked up and gave a small smile. Lily wanted to fix the problem together.

After that, Lily asked her friend to make a compromise. They agreed to take turns pushing each other on the swing. Lily pushed her friend first to show she was sorry. Her friend pushed Lily for the same amount of time.

Finally, both girls felt happy about the apology. They learned that saying sorry and making a compromise can fix a problem. Lily and her friend played together for the rest of the afternoon.
