[Ninereeds]First, Noah brings his friends to the garden to plant vegetables. Noah digs holes in the soil with a shovel because the ground is hard. His friends place seeds in each hole and cover them with dirt. If everyone helps with the planting, then the work will go faster. The children work together as a team.

Noah carries a bucket of water from the tap to the garden rows. His friends water each row side by side in the warm afternoon sun. "If we give the seeds water every day," Noah says, "then they will grow strong." The children nod and share the effort of the task.

After a few weeks, tiny green shoots push through the soil. The children smile because their hard work helped the garden grow. Noah shows them how to pull weeds so the plants get enough sun and moisture.

Finally, tall vegetables fill the garden rows. The children pick the vegetables together and feel proud. Unity helps them build something good that none could make alone. Noah feels happy because their teamwork made the garden strong.
