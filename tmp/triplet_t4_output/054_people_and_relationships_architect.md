[Ninereeds]First, Ella began her design for the new library by studying how the community would use the space. She visited other libraries and talked with families about the rooms they liked best. If the building felt warm and open, she thought, then more people would visit and stay to read. She drew careful plans with large windows that would fill the rooms with morning light.

After the construction was finished, Ella walked through the front doors with a group of children. A girl touched a wooden table and said, "Did you plan all of this yourself?" Ella smiled and said, "I made the plans, but many workers helped turn the design into something real." The children explored the quiet reading room where soft light fell across the shelves.

After that, Ella watched families fill the tables in the afternoon. Parents read stories to their children in the cozy chairs near the windows. If the library gave people a comfortable place to share stories, she realized, then it would bring the whole neighborhood together.

Finally, Ella stood near the entrance and looked at her finished creation. The building stood open and welcoming for everyone who came through the doors. She felt proud because her design had brought so many people together in one peaceful space.
