[Ninereeds]Noah brings friends to the garden to plant vegetables. Noah digs holes in the soil with a shovel because the ground is hard. His friends place seeds in each hole and cover them with dirt. The children work together because they want a strong garden.

"Can everyone help water the garden?" Noah asks his friends. The children nod and carry water from the tap so the seeds get moisture. They water every row side by side in the warm afternoon sun. Noah feels happy because each child gives effort to the task.

After a few weeks, tiny green shoots push through the soil. The children smile because their hard work helped the garden grow. Unity helps them build something good that none could make alone. Noah feels proud of their teamwork.
