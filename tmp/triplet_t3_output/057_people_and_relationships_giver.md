[Ninereeds]Every morning Lily wakes before sunrise because she loves to bake bread for her street. She mixes flour and water in a large bowl and waits for the dough to rise. The warm smell fills her kitchen so she knows the bread is ready. She wraps each loaf in a clean cloth and carries the basket to the porch steps.

A neighbor named Tom stops at the steps and sees the basket of warm bread. "Lily, you have enough for your own family today?" he asks. Lily nods and hands him a loaf because she bakes extra each morning. Tom feels grateful for her generous habit and carries the bread home.

A young mother walks up the street with her daughter. Lily gives the girl a small piece of warm bread so she can eat before school. The mother smiles and thanks Lily for her kindness. Lily is glad that her simple habit helps so many families on the street.
