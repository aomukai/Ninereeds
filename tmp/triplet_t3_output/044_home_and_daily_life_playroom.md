[Ninereeds]Lily walks into the playroom after school because the afternoon sunlight makes the mess easy to see. Blocks are scattered across the floor near the window. Dolls lie in a pile beside the bookshelf. The room looks very untidy from a full day of play.

"This room needs a big cleanup before dinner," Lily says to her little brother. He nods and picks up a red block because it is closest to the basket. "I will put the dolls on the shelf," he offers, so Lily hands him a stack of them. They work together in the warm light.

The daily routine of sorting and sweeping brings order to the playroom. Lily pushes the broom across the floor until every corner is clean. She places the broom back in the closet because the room is finally neat. The playroom is ready for another day of fun.
