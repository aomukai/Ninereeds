Lily looked over and noticed her friend waiting alone. Lily walked to the bench and said, "I am sorry for not sharing the swing." Her friend looked up and gave a small smile because she wanted to feel better too. Lily asked if they could make a compromise so both could play.

They agreed to take turns pushing each other on the swing. Lily pushed her friend first because Lily wanted to show she was sorry. Then her friend pushed Lily for the same amount of time. They both felt happy about the apology because they worked together to fix the problem.
