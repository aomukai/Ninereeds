[Ninereeds]Cody walked to the pond early because he wanted to see the tall grey bird again. The bird stood still in the shallow water and waited for a fish to swim near its long legs. Cody held his breath and watched the bird's sharp beak.

"Why does it stand so still?" Cody whispered to himself. The bird did not move because it knew that fish swam past still things without fear. A small silver creature glided near the bird's legs. The bird's beak darted down in a fast pounce and grabbed the silver fish. "That was so quick!" Cody said.

The bird lifted its head and swallowed the fresh meal whole. Cody smiled because he understood that the beak was a perfect tool for catching slippery fish. The bird waded deeper into the pond so it could find another fish to eat.
