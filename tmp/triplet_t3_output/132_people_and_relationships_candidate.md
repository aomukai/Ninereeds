[Ninereeds]Ella is a candidate who knocks on doors in the neighborhood because she wants to hear what the voters need. She listens to their concerns about the school. The campaign needs many days of hard work, so Ella feels tired from all the work. Her supporters help her with the speeches and the ballots.

Ella waits at the podium in the hall. "I hope I have done enough," she says to the crowd. "The choice is yours." Each voter places a ballot in the box. Ella watches the votes and feels nervous because the result is not yet known.

The votes are counted and the numbers say that Ella has won the election. The approval of the community feels like a great reward. She shakes hands with every voter and thanks them for their trust. Ella feels proud of the day.
