[Ninereeds]A child felt lonely because the other children were playing games without them. The exclusion from the group made the morning feel long and empty. The child sat on a bench near the playground and watched the other children run past. Part of the child wanted to ask to join, but the words would not come out.

Cody walked over and sat down beside the child. "Do you want to come make clay shapes with me?" Cody asked. The child felt nervous because the invitation felt unexpected. "You can make it again if it falls," Cody said. The child said yes, so the two of them walked to the art table together.

A glad feeling spread through the child because Cody had offered a way to belong. They sat down and began to shape small bowls and animals from the soft clay. The child was laughing as a little clay bird began to wobble and fall over. Cody showed the child how to roll the clay into long smooth coils. A wide smile spread across the child's face because the day had changed.
