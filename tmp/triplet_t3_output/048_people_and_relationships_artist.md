[Ninereeds]The artist sits at a table by the window. Morning light falls across the blank canvas because the sun rises through the glass. A knock comes at the door, and Nora steps in with a cup of coffee. She sets it beside the paint tray and smiles.

"You cannot find the right idea while you wait," Nora says. "So you must start and let the work teach you." The artist nods because Nora is often right. The artist picks up a brush and mixes color on a plate. She makes the first stroke and the canvas is no longer blank.

The artist keeps working because the shapes begin to feel true. The creation grows with each layer of light and shadow. Nora watches from the chair as inspiration fills the room. The artist feels a steady sense of purpose and does not stop until the painting is done.
