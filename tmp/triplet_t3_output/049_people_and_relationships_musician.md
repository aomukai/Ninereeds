[Ninereeds]Owen practiced his violin piece with great care because he wanted his performance to be right. On the evening of the show, he carried his instrument to the small hall and stepped onto the stage. A woman in the front row smiled at him, so Owen took a slow breath and lifted his bow. His heart beat with a steady rhythm as he waited for the room to grow still.

"I hope you enjoy this tune," Owen said with a warm grin. The audience gave a gentle clap as he began to play. The notes bounced like playful children across the room, and a happy laugh rose from the crowd. Owen kept a clear smile because he could feel the joy in the sound. The music filled the whole hall with a bright glow.

After the song ended, the room rose into great applause. Owen bowed and said, "Thank you. That was a real joy." The laughter and clapping moved through the crowd for a long moment. Owen walked off the stage with his violin in hand and a grin across his face.
