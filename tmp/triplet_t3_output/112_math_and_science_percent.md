[Ninereeds]Noah sat at his desk when the teacher handed back the graded math test. He looked at his score and counted his correct answers. He saw nine correct answers out of ten questions in total. Noah knew he needed to find the percent from that total. He decided to use division to solve the problem.

Noah performed the division on a piece of paper. He divided nine by ten and got zero point nine. That result showed him that his score equaled ninety percent. "So a percent compares a part to a total of one hundred?" Noah asked. The teacher smiled and said, "Yes, every percent shows a number out of one hundred."

Because Noah checked his work with the same division, he knew the answer was correct. He felt happy because he knew what the percent meant. Noah wrote ninety percent at the top of his paper. He circled his score with a green mark.
