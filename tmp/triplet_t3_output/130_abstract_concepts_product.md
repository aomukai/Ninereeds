[Ninereeds]Will studied his design on the workbench because he wanted every measurement to be exact. He traced the drawing of the wooden box with his pencil and marked each cut line carefully. The design showed how all the pieces would fit together, so he knew the joints would be tight.

"Are you ready for the production run today?" his helper asked. Will nodded because the materials were already laid out on the bench. He picked up his saw and cut each piece along the marked lines. Sanding the rough edges took time, so he worked slowly until every surface felt smooth.

After the glue dried on the seams, he wiped the dust from the box and applied a coat of oil. The wood glowed with a warm shine because the oil brought out the grain. Will placed his finished product on the table and smiled. It stood sturdy and ready for its new home.
