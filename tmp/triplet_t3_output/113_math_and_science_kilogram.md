[Ninereeds]Nora stood in the kitchen beside the digital scale. She placed a bag of flour on it. The scale showed one kilogram. She wondered why the bag weighed that much. Her mother explained that one kilogram equals one thousand grams.

Nora filled a container with one liter of water. "Does this weigh one kilogram too?" she asked. Her mother placed it on the scale because water has a known density. The scale showed exactly one kilogram, so Nora smiled.

Nora poured the water out and filled the same container with oil. The scale showed a lower number because oil is less dense. She understood that the same volume can hold different amounts of mass. Weight depends on what a substance is made of.
