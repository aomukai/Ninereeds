### Known issue: aug_done.txt path staleness
`aug_done.txt` stores entries as `wave:bucket/filename`. When files move between buckets (e.g., `unsorted/` → `actions/`), old entries don't match new paths. Workers treat moved files as not-done and regenerate. **Fix for future aug passes:** clear aug_done.txt after any bucket reorganisation, or rebuild it from the actual rephrase files on disk.
