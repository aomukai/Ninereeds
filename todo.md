## Wiki Level 3 Plan

Level 3 Wiki articles move beyond Q&A collections into cohesive 150-250 word prose articles (or long-form responses) that explicitly model causal and contrastive reasoning.

### Level 3 Requirements
- **Length:** 150-250 words per expanded entry.
- **Connectives:** Mandatory use of "because," "so," "if...then," and "but."
- **Structure:** 2-3 paragraphs grouping related facts and reasoning chains.
- **Content:** Every expanded entry must include at least one explicit causal chain and one contrast/comparison.

### Candidate Set (Reconfirmed 2026-04-27)
1. **emotions_entries.md** (Batch 1): Chosen as the primary vertical slice for internal reasoning (feelings driven by events).
2. **perspective_taking_and_theory_of_mind_entries.md** (Batch 2): High value for social reasoning (beliefs/desires driving action).
3. **evidence_and_justification_entries.md** (Batch 2): High value for logical reasoning (supporting claims with reasons).

All other Level 2 files remain capped at Level 2 for now to keep the expansion focused on the highest-leverage reasoning domains.
