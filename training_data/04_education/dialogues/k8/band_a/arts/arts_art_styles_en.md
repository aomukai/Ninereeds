[user]  
Sculptures are different. A sculpture is a three‑dimensional artwork you can walk around and see from all sides, like a statue made of stone or clay. A drawing or painting is flat—you can only see it from the front. So a sculpture isn’t the same as a drawing, even though both are art.

It’s not the same as sculpture because a building has to work for people, while a sculpture’s main job is to express an idea.
