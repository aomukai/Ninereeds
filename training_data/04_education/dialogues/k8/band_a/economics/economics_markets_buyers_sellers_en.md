When I buy a toy, how does the price get decided?  

[user]  
The price is what a buyer pays a seller. It tells you how much that toy is worth in that market. If many sellers sell the same toy, they might compete to get you to buy from them.  

[Ninereeds]  
What does competition do to the price?  

[user]  
When many sellers compete, they often lower the price so you will choose their toy instead of another seller’s. That’s why you can sometimes find a bargain—a lower price than usual.
