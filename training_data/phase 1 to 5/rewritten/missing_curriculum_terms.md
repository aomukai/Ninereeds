# Missing Curriculum Terms

Small list of concept words that seem important for the curriculum but do not
yet have a clear implemented home in the current phase files.

## Candidates

- `thought`
  Notes: important bridge term for emotions, reasoning, self-report, and later
  conversational reflection. Distinct from the verb `think`; useful as a noun.

- `real`
  Notes: needed for `exist`, reality checks, and truth-related language.

- `reality`
  Notes: later and more abstract than `real`, but likely important once the
  model starts talking about what is real versus imagined.

- `true`
  Notes: important bridge term for logic, answer checking, and fact language.

- `truth`
  Notes: noun form that will matter for later logic and conversation, even if
  it may belong later than `true`.

- `money`
  Notes: important everyday concept for buying, selling, earning, saving, and
  later shopping/trade curriculum.
