# Safety Signs and Symbols Entries

[user]what is a sign?
[assistant]A sign is something people look at to get information or a warning. A sign may use words, colors, or pictures to tell people what to do. Signs help people stay safe and find the right place. A sign is not the same as a toy.

[user]what is a symbol?
[assistant]A symbol is a picture or mark that stands for an idea or message. A symbol can show danger, help, fire, or another important meaning. Symbols help people understand signs quickly. A symbol is not the same as a sentence.

[user]what is a stop sign?
[assistant]A stop sign is a road sign that tells drivers to stop. A stop sign is red and is often found at corners or crossings. Stop signs help cars wait and help keep people safe. A stop sign is not the same as a green light.

[user]what is an exit sign?
[assistant]An exit sign shows where people can go out of a building. People look for an exit sign in schools, stores, and theaters. An exit sign helps people find the way out quickly. An exit sign is not the same as a wall decoration.

[user]what is a danger sign?
[assistant]A danger sign warns that a place or thing could hurt people. A danger sign may be used near deep water, strong machines, or electric power. A danger sign tells people to stay alert and be careful. A danger sign is not the same as a welcome sign.

[user]what is a caution sign?
[assistant]A caution sign tells people to slow down and be careful. A caution sign may be used for a wet floor or another risky spot. A caution sign helps people notice a problem before they get hurt. A caution sign is not the same as a stop sign.

[user]what is a no entry sign?
[assistant]A no entry sign tells people not to go into a place. A no entry sign may be placed on a gate, a door, or a road. A no entry sign helps keep people out of unsafe or private places. A no entry sign is not the same as an exit sign.

[user]what is a pedestrian crossing sign?
[assistant]A pedestrian crossing sign shows a place where people may walk across a road. Drivers who see the sign should watch for people crossing. A pedestrian crossing sign helps walkers and drivers share the road safely. A pedestrian crossing sign is not the same as a speed sign.

[user]what is a poison symbol?
[assistant]A poison symbol warns that something could make a person very sick or hurt the body. A poison symbol may be found on dangerous cleaners or other harmful things. A poison symbol tells people not to touch, taste, or drink what is inside. A poison symbol is not the same as a recycling symbol.

[user]what is a first aid symbol?
[assistant]A first aid symbol shows where people can find basic medical help or supplies. A first aid symbol may mark a kit with bandages and other tools for small injuries. A first aid symbol helps people find help quickly. A first aid symbol is not the same as a poison symbol.

[user]what is a slippery floor sign?
[assistant]A slippery floor sign warns that the floor may be wet and easy to slip on. People often see this sign after a floor has been cleaned. A slippery floor sign tells people to walk carefully. A slippery floor sign is not the same as a welcome sign.

[user]what is a recycling symbol?
[assistant]A recycling symbol is a mark made of three arrows forming a circle that shows an item can be recycled. People look for the recycling symbol on paper, bottles, and other materials. The recycling symbol helps people know what to put in a recycling bin. A recycling symbol is not the same as a poison symbol.

[user]what is a speed limit sign?
[assistant]A speed limit sign is a road sign that shows the highest speed a vehicle may travel on that road. Drivers look at speed limit signs to know how fast they are allowed to go. Speed limit signs help keep roads safer for both drivers and walkers. A speed limit sign is not the same as a stop sign.

[user]what is a school zone sign?
[assistant]A school zone sign is a road sign that tells drivers to slow down near a school. The sign helps protect children who are arriving at school or crossing the road nearby. Drivers must be extra careful in a school zone. A school zone sign is not the same as a pedestrian crossing sign.
