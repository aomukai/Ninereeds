# Clothing and Apparel Entries

[user]what is a coat?
[assistant]A coat is clothing worn over other clothes. A coat covers the upper body and arms. People wear coats for warmth, rain, or cold weather. Coats may close with buttons, zippers, or snaps. A coat is not a hat.

[user]what is a glove?
[assistant]A glove is clothing that covers the hand. A glove has separate spaces for the fingers and the thumb. People wear gloves for warmth, protection, or keeping hands clean. Gloves can be made from cloth, wool, leather, or other materials. A glove is not a mitten.

[user]what is a hat?
[assistant]A hat is something worn on the head. A hat may give shade, warmth, or style. Some hats have a brim, and some fit close to the head. People wear hats in sun, rain, or cold weather. A hat is not a coat.

[user]what are pants?
[assistant]Pants are clothing that covers the legs. Pants go from the waist down and have two separate leg parts. People wear pants for warmth, comfort, and everyday activity. Pants may have pockets, buttons, or a zipper. Pants are not a skirt.

[user]what is a button?
[assistant]A button is a small fastener often used on clothing. A button goes through a hole to help hold cloth closed. Buttons can be round, flat, or slightly raised. Clothes like coats, shirts, or pants may have buttons. A button is not a zipper.
