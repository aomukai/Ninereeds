# Clothing and Apparel Entries

[user]what is clothing?
[assistant]Clothing means things people wear on the body. Shirts, pants, coats, hats, socks, and shoes are all clothing. People wear clothing for warmth, comfort, work, weather, and daily life. Different kinds of clothing cover different body parts. Clothing is not skin.

[user]what is a coat?
[assistant]A coat is clothing worn over other clothes. A coat covers the upper body and arms. People wear coats for warmth, rain, or cold weather. Coats may close with buttons, zippers, or snaps. A coat is not a hat.

[user]what is a jacket?
[assistant]A jacket is a short coat. A jacket covers the upper body and arms. People wear jackets for warmth, wind, or light rain. A jacket is often shorter and lighter than a long coat. A jacket is not a coat.

[user]what is a shirt?
[assistant]A shirt is clothing worn on the upper body. A shirt may have short sleeves, long sleeves, buttons, or a collar. People wear shirts every day at home, school, or work. A shirt is not pants.

[user]what is a hat?
[assistant]A hat is something worn on the head. A hat may give shade, warmth, or style. Some hats have a brim, and some fit close to the head. People wear hats in sun, rain, or cold weather. A hat is not a coat.

[user]what is a glove?
[assistant]A glove is clothing that covers the hand. A glove has separate spaces for the fingers and the thumb. People wear gloves for warmth, protection, or keeping hands clean. A glove is not a mitten.

[user]what is a mitten?
[assistant]A mitten is clothing for the hand. A mitten has one part for the thumb and one larger part for the fingers together. People often wear mittens in cold weather. A mitten is not a glove.

[user]what are pants?
[assistant]Pants are clothing that covers the legs. Pants go from the waist down and have two separate leg parts. People wear pants for warmth, comfort, and everyday activity. Pants may have pockets, buttons, or a zipper. Pants are not a skirt.

[user]what is a skirt?
[assistant]A skirt is clothing worn from the waist down. A skirt hangs down around the legs without separate leg parts. Skirts can be short or long and are made in many styles. A skirt is not pants.

[user]what is a dress?
[assistant]A dress is one piece of clothing that covers the body from the shoulders down. A dress may be short or long and may have sleeves or no sleeves. People wear dresses in many colors and styles. A dress is not a shirt.

[user]what is a sock?
[assistant]A sock is clothing worn on the foot. Socks help keep feet warm and can make shoes feel more comfortable. Socks are often soft and made from cloth or wool. A sock is not a shoe.

[user]what is a shoe?
[assistant]A shoe is something worn on the foot for walking and protection. Shoes help protect feet from rough ground, cold, water, or sharp things. Many shoes have laces, straps, or other fasteners. A shoe is not a sock.

[user]what is a button?
[assistant]A button is a small fastener often used on clothing. A button goes through a hole to help hold cloth closed. Buttons can be round, flat, or slightly raised. Clothes like coats, shirts, or pants may have buttons. A button is not a zipper.

[user]what is a zipper?
[assistant]A zipper is a fastener used to open and close clothing or bags. A zipper has two sides that join together when a small slider moves along them. Coats, pants, and backpacks may have zippers. A zipper is not a button.

[user]what is a scarf?
[assistant]A scarf is a long piece of cloth worn around the neck or head. People wear scarves to stay warm in cold weather. A scarf can also be worn for style. A scarf is not the same as a hat.

[user]what are boots?
[assistant]Boots are shoes that go higher than the ankle. People wear boots in rain, mud, snow, or cold weather. Boots help protect the feet and lower legs. Boots are not the same as shoes.

[user]what is a belt?
[assistant]A belt is a strip of cloth or leather worn around the waist. A belt helps hold pants in place. Belts may have a buckle in the middle. A belt is not the same as a zipper.

[user]what is a collar?
[assistant]A collar is the part of a shirt or coat that goes around the neck. A collar may lie flat, stand up, or fold over. Collars are found on many kinds of clothing. A collar is not the same as a sleeve.

[user]what is a sleeve?
[assistant]A sleeve is the part of a shirt or coat that covers an arm. A sleeve can be short, reaching the elbow, or long, reaching the wrist. Sleeves help keep arms warm or covered. A sleeve is not the same as a collar.
