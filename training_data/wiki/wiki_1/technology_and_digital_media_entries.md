[Ninereeds]A tablet is a flat device with a screen that people touch to use. Children may read, draw, play games, or watch videos on a tablet. A tablet is often bigger than a phone and smaller than many computers. Tablets use apps and touch controls. A tablet is not a phone.

[user]what is a computer?

[user]what is a screen?
[Ninereeds]A screen is the part of a device that shows words, pictures, or video. Phones, tablets, computers, and TVs all have screens. People look at a screen to see what the device is doing. Some screens can also be touched to give commands. A screen is not a keyboard.

[user]what is an app?
[Ninereeds]An app is a program people use on a phone or tablet. One app may be for drawing, another for games, and another for messages. Apps help a device do different jobs. Children often know apps by their pictures and names. An app is not a message.

[user]what is a video?
[Ninereeds]A video is moving pictures shown on a screen. Videos can show stories, lessons, games, songs, or real events. People may watch a video for fun or for learning. A video changes over time instead of staying still like one picture. A video is not a photo.

[user]what is a message?
[Ninereeds]A message is words or signs sent from one person to another. A message can be spoken, written, or sent by phone or computer. People send messages to share news, ask questions, or stay in touch. A message may be short or long. A message is not a call.

[Ninereeds]Swipe means move a finger across a screen to make something happen. A child may swipe to move to the next picture or unlock a device. Swiping is a common touch action on phones and tablets. The finger moves across the glass instead of pressing a key. Swipe is not tap.

[user]what does tap mean?
[Ninereeds]Tap means touch a screen quickly with a finger. A child may tap an app, a picture, or a button on a device. Tapping tells the device what to open or do. Tap is one of the simplest screen actions. Tap is not swipe.

[user]what is a TV?

[user]what is a keyboard?

[user]what is a photo?
