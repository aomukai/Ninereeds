[user]what is a shape?
[Ninereeds]A shape is the form or outline of something. Balls, boxes, and signs all have shapes. Shapes can be drawn on paper or seen in the world. Shape helps people notice and describe how something looks. A shape is not a color.

[user]what is a circle?
[Ninereeds]A circle is a round flat shape with no corners. Coins, wheels, and clocks can be circles. A circle is smooth all around the edge. A circle looks round from every side. A circle is not a square.

[user]what does it mean for something to be "round"?
[Ninereeds]Round means shaped like a circle or curved all around. A ball, a coin, and the full moon can look round. Round things do not have sharp corners. Round is a word people use for both flat shapes and solid things. Round is not the same as square.

[user]what is a square?
[Ninereeds]A square is a flat shape with four equal sides and four corners. Tiles, windows, and some boxes are square-shaped. All four sides of a square are the same length. A square has straight edges, not a round edge. A square is not a circle.

[user]what is a triangle?
[Ninereeds]A triangle is a flat shape with three sides and three corners. Triangles can be big or small, but they always have three sides. Rooftops and slices of pizza can look like triangles. A triangle has fewer sides than a square. A triangle is not a square.

[user]what is a rectangle?
[Ninereeds]A rectangle is a flat shape with four sides and four corners. Doors, books, and screens are often rectangles. The opposite sides of a rectangle match in length. A rectangle is like a square, but not all sides have to be the same. A rectangle is not a triangle.

[user]what is addition?
[Ninereeds]Addition is a mathematical operation that combines two numbers into one total. Putting three apples together with two more apples gives five apples. Counting the total of a group of objects uses this process. Addition is not subtraction.

[user]what is an angle?
[Ninereeds]An angle is the space between two lines that meet at a point. A corner of a book makes a small space. A door opened half way makes a wide opening. An angle can be narrow or wide. Angle is not a line.

[user]what is an area?
[Ninereeds]An area is a space on a surface. A kitchen is a room for making food. A playground holds space for games. Different places have different shapes and sizes. Area is not an object.

[user]what is a column?
[Ninereeds]A column is a tall, upright part of a building that helps hold up a roof. The vertical parts stand in rows to support walkways and porches. Some parts are made from stone, and some are made from wood. Many ancient temples have columns at the entrance. The form is often round and straight. A column is not a wall.

[user]what is a cube?
[Ninereeds]A cube is a solid shape with six square faces. Every face is a flat square of equal size. A dice used in games is one example of a cube. Some toy building blocks are also cubes. Cube is not a round sphere.

[user]what is a cylinder?
[Ninereeds]A cylinder is a three-dimensional form with a round top and a round bottom. The sides are straight and flat. A soda can has this shape. A pipe also has this shape. Cylinder is not a box.

[user]what is a division?
[Ninereeds]Division is a math operation that splits a number into equal parts. For example, twelve divided by three gives four pieces. Each piece has the same amount. A baker can use division to share eight cookies among four children. Division is not addition.

[user]what is a fewer?
[Ninereeds]Fewer is a word that people use to describe a smaller number of things. This word can show that difference when the count of items is small. For example, three apples is fewer than eight apples. Fewer is not more.

[user]what is a figure?
[Ninereeds]A figure is a shape or form that people see or make. A circle is one kind of figure. A square has straight lines. A triangle has three sides. Figure is not a real object.

[user]what is math?
[Ninereeds]Math is a way of working with numbers and shapes. Counting a group of apples shows how numbers work. Measuring the length of a table uses shapes and numbers. People use math every day to solve simple problems. Math is not a story.

[user]what is a percent?
[Ninereeds]Percent means per hundred. This word shows a part out of one hundred equal parts. For example, a score of eight correct answers out of ten questions is eighty percent. This way of showing parts helps people compare amounts in the same way. percent is not a guess.

[user]what is rotation?
[Ninereeds]Rotation is a circular motion around a center point. A top spins around a center point when a person gives the top a turn. The earth rotates once each day and night. A wheel rotates around a center axle to help a cart move forward. Rotation is not a straight line in one direction.

[user]what does single mean?

[user]what is a sphere?
[Ninereeds]A sphere is a round shape. Every point on the surface sits at the same distance from the center. A ball and a globe are both this shape. The earth looks round like this from space. sphere is not a cube.

[user]what is subtraction?
[Ninereeds]Subtraction is a math operation that takes one number away from another number. Subtraction finds the difference between two groups of things. For example, taking 3 apples away from 5 apples leaves 2 apples. Another example shows 4 cookies in a pile, and eating 1 cookie leaves 3 cookies. Subtraction is not addition.

[user]what is a total?

The combined scores from every question make one answer called a grade. Total brings separate pieces into one single full amount. Total is not a part.
