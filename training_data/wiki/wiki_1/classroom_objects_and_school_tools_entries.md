# Classroom Objects and School Tools Entries

[user]what is a school tool?
[Ninereeds]A school tool is an object children use for learning or school work. School tools help with writing, cutting, measuring, drawing, and organizing. Many school tools are used in classrooms every day. A school tool helps a student do a task more easily. A school tool is not the same as a toy.

[user]what is a ruler?
[Ninereeds]A ruler is a straight school tool used for measuring or drawing lines. A ruler often has numbers and small marks along the edge. Children use rulers in math, drawing, and other class work. A ruler can help make a line neat and straight. A ruler is not a pencil.

[user]what is an eraser?
[Ninereeds]An eraser is a small tool used to remove pencil marks. Children use an eraser to fix writing or change a drawing. An eraser helps a student try again without throwing the paper away. Some erasers are attached to pencils, and some are separate. An eraser is not a crayon.

[user]what is glue?
[Ninereeds]Glue is something used to stick things together. Children use glue with paper, pictures, and small craft materials. Glue can come as a liquid or as a glue stick. Glue helps join parts so they stay together. Glue is not the same as tape.

[user]what are scissors?
[Ninereeds]Scissors are a cutting tool with two blades that move around a center point. Children use scissors to cut paper, cloth, or other classroom materials. Scissors should be used with care. Scissors help shape and prepare materials for school work and art. Scissors are not a ruler.

[user]what is a marker?
[Ninereeds]A marker is a writing and drawing tool that makes bold lines with ink. Markers may be used on paper, posters, or whiteboards. Many markers come in bright colors. A marker often makes darker and thicker lines than a pencil. A marker is not a crayon.

[user]what is a whiteboard?
[Ninereeds]A whiteboard is a smooth board people write on with special markers. Teachers may write words, numbers, or drawings on a whiteboard during a lesson. The writing can be erased and changed many times. Whiteboards are common in classrooms. A whiteboard is not paper.

[user]what is a glue stick?
[Ninereeds]A glue stick is a solid kind of glue in a tube. Children twist the bottom to push the glue up before using it. A glue stick is often neat and easy for paper work. It helps stick flat things together without much mess. A glue stick is not the same as a marker.

[user]what is a sharpener?
[Ninereeds]A sharpener is a tool used to make a pencil point again. A child puts the pencil into the sharpener and turns or presses it. Sharpening helps a pencil write more neatly. Some sharpeners hold the wood and pencil dust inside. A sharpener is not an eraser.
