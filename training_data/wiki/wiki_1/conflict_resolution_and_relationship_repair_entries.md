[user]what is conflict resolution?
[Ninereeds]Conflict resolution is fixing a problem between people after a conflict or argument. People may use calm words, listening, and fair choices during conflict resolution. Conflict resolution helps a group or friendship work again. The goal is not just stopping noise but repairing the problem. Conflict resolution is not the same as staying stuck in the conflict.

[user]what does compromise mean?
[Ninereeds]Compromise means each side gives up a little so both sides can move forward. Children may compromise about a game, a turn, or what to do next. A compromise is not perfect for only one person. It helps solve some conflicts in a fairer way. Compromise is not the same as refusing everything.

[user]what does forgive mean?
[Ninereeds]To forgive means to stop holding on to anger after another person made a mistake or caused hurt. Forgiving can happen after an apology, a repair, or honest change. Forgiving does not mean the hurt was fine, but it can help a relationship heal. People may need time before forgiving. To forgive is not the same as staying angry forever.

[user]what does apologize mean?
[Ninereeds]To apologize means to say sorry in a serious and responsible way after doing something wrong. An apology may include naming the mistake and trying to make things better. People apologize to help repair trust and feelings. A real apology is more than a quick word with no care. To apologize is not the same as blaming the other person.

[user]what does let's try again mean?
[Ninereeds]Let's try again is something people say when they want to start over after a mistake, conflict, or failed try. The phrase can help lower tension and open a better second try. Children may say let's try again during a game, a talk, or group work. It points toward repair instead of giving up. Let's try again is not the same as keep fighting.

[user]what does that's okay mean?

People often say that's okay when they do not want the problem to keep growing. The phrase helps close some small social repairs. That's okay is not the same as I am still upset.

[user]what does how can we fix this mean?
[Ninereeds]How can we fix this is a question people ask when they want to solve a problem together. A child may ask this after a mistake, an argument, or broken trust. The question turns attention toward repair and action. It invites both sides to think about a solution. How can we fix this is not the same as whose fault is this.

[user]what is a clash?
[Ninereeds]A clash is when two people or groups disagree with each other. The disagreement can be a loud argument between people. The disagreement can also be a fight between groups. A sports game can become a clash when two teams want the same thing. clash is not a peaceful agreement.

[user]what is an issue?
[Ninereeds]An issue is a subject that people talk about or need to work on. A problem that needs a fix can be this kind of thing. For example, a broken toy in a classroom is a matter that needs care.
