# Animal Habitats and Homes Entries

[user]what is an animal habitat?
[assistant]An animal habitat is the kind of place where an animal lives. A habitat gives an animal food, water, shelter, and space. Forests, ponds, deserts, and reefs can all be habitats. Different animals need different habitats to live well. An animal habitat is not an animal home.

[user]what is an animal home?
[assistant]An animal home is the place where an animal rests, hides, or raises young. An animal home is often part of a larger habitat. A nest, den, burrow, hive, or web can be an animal home. Animal homes help animals stay safe. An animal home is not an animal habitat.

[user]what is a nest?
[assistant]A nest is a home that many birds build for eggs and young birds. Nests may be made from sticks, grass, mud, or leaves. Many nests sit in trees, bushes, or on ledges. A nest helps hold eggs in one safe place. A nest is not a burrow.

[user]what is a burrow?
[assistant]A burrow is a hole or tunnel in the ground where an animal lives or hides. Rabbits, foxes, and other animals can use burrows. A burrow can keep an animal cool, hidden, and protected from weather. Some burrows have more than one tunnel or room. A burrow is not a nest.

[user]what is a den?
[assistant]A den is a sheltered place where some animals rest or raise young. Bears, foxes, and wolves may use a den. A den can be in the ground, in rocks, or in thick plants. A den helps an animal stay hidden and safe. A den is not a hive.

[user]what is a hive?
[assistant]A hive is the home of bees. A hive holds many bees together in one place. Bees make and use the inside of a hive to raise young and store honey. A hive can hang in a tree or live in a box made by people. A hive is not a den.

[user]what is a web?
[assistant]A web is a silk home or trap made by a spider. A spider web can be stretched between branches, corners, or other supports. The web helps the spider catch insects and stay in one place. Some webs are round, and some are shaped in other ways. A web is not a nest.

[user]what is a reef?
[assistant]A reef is a rocky or coral place in the sea where many animals live. Fish, crabs, and many other sea animals can find food and shelter in a reef. Reefs are full of small spaces for hiding and resting. A reef is a busy water habitat. A reef is not a pond.
