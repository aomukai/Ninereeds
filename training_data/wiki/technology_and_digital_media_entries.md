# Technology and Digital Media Entries

[user]what is technology?
[assistant]Technology is tools and systems people make to do jobs, solve problems, or share information. Phones, computers, and tablets are kinds of technology. Technology can help people learn, talk, play, and work. Many children use technology at home and at school. Technology is not a phone.

[user]what is a phone?
[assistant]A phone is a device people use to talk, send messages, and use apps. Some phones fit in a pocket and have a screen. A phone can also take pictures, play videos, or show maps. Many phones connect people who are far apart. A phone is not a tablet.

[user]what is a tablet?
[assistant]A tablet is a flat device with a screen that people touch to use. Children may read, draw, play games, or watch videos on a tablet. A tablet is often bigger than a phone and smaller than many computers. Tablets use apps and touch controls. A tablet is not a phone.

[user]what is a computer?
[assistant]A computer is a device used for writing, learning, games, and many kinds of work. Computers can have a screen, keyboard, and mouse. People use computers to make things, find information, and communicate. Some computers are small and some are large. A computer is not a TV.

[user]what is a screen?
[assistant]A screen is the part of a device that shows words, pictures, or video. Phones, tablets, computers, and TVs all have screens. People look at a screen to see what the device is doing. Some screens can also be touched to give commands. A screen is not a keyboard.

[user]what is an app?
[assistant]An app is a program people use on a phone or tablet. One app may be for drawing, another for games, and another for messages. Apps help a device do different jobs. Children often know apps by their pictures and names. An app is not a message.

[user]what is a video?
[assistant]A video is moving pictures shown on a screen. Videos can show stories, lessons, games, songs, or real events. People may watch a video for fun or for learning. A video changes over time instead of staying still like one picture. A video is not a photo.

[user]what is a message?
[assistant]A message is words or signs sent from one person to another. A message can be spoken, written, or sent by phone or computer. People send messages to share news, ask questions, or stay in touch. A message may be short or long. A message is not a call.

[user]what does swipe mean?
[assistant]Swipe means move a finger across a screen to make something happen. A child may swipe to move to the next picture or unlock a device. Swiping is a common touch action on phones and tablets. The finger moves across the glass instead of pressing a key. Swipe is not tap.

[user]what does tap mean?
[assistant]Tap means touch a screen quickly with a finger. A child may tap an app, a picture, or a button on a device. Tapping tells the device what to open or do. Tap is one of the simplest screen actions. Tap is not swipe.

[user]what is a TV?
[assistant]A TV is a large screen device used for watching shows, films, and news. A TV is often placed in a room and watched by a group of people. People use a remote or buttons to change what is playing. A TV shows video and sound together. A TV is not a computer.

[user]what is a keyboard?
[assistant]A keyboard is a set of keys that people press to type letters, numbers, and symbols. People use keyboards with computers to write, search, and give commands. Each key makes a different character or action. Some keyboards are attached to computers and some are separate. A keyboard is not a screen.

[user]what is a photo?
[assistant]A photo is one still picture taken by a camera or phone. A photo captures one moment in time and does not move or change. People take photos to remember people, places, and special moments. Photos can be shared on screens or printed on paper. A photo is not a video.

[user]what is a username?
[assistant]A username is a name a person chooses to use on an app, game, or website. A username helps a device or other people know who is using it. Some usernames are real names and some are made-up names. A username is often used with a password to log in. A username is not a password.
