[user]what is a clam?
[assistant]A clam is a water animal with two hard shells. A clam can open and close its shell to protect its soft body. Many clams live in sand or mud under shallow water. Clams feed by taking tiny bits of food from the water. A clam is not a crab.

[user]what is a coral?
[assistant]Coral is a small sea animal that lives with many others in a group. Coral animals build hard homes that join together and grow into coral reefs. Many fish and other sea animals hide or feed near coral. Coral grows best in warm shallow sea water. Coral is not a plant.

[user]what is a crab?
[assistant]A crab is a water animal with a hard shell and claws. Crabs often walk sideways on their legs. Many crabs live near the sea, on the shore, or in shallow water. Crabs eat small animals, plants, or bits of food they find. A crab is not a fish.

[user]what is a fish?
[assistant]A fish is an animal that lives in water. Fish use fins to swim and gills to breathe in water. Many fish have scales covering the body. Fish can live in rivers, lakes, ponds, or the sea. A fish is not a dolphin.

[user]what is a jellyfish?
[assistant]A jellyfish is a soft water animal with a round bell-shaped body. Long tentacles hang down from the body and may sting. Jellyfish drift and pulse through the water. Many jellyfish live in the sea and eat tiny water animals. A jellyfish is not a fish.

[user]what is an octopus?
[assistant]An octopus is a soft sea animal with eight arms. An octopus can squeeze into small places and hide well. It can change color to help it blend in with rocks or plants. Octopuses hunt animals like crabs and fish for food. An octopus is not a starfish.

[user]what is a salmon?
[assistant]A salmon is a fish that can live in rivers and in the sea. Many salmon begin life in a river, swim out to sea, and later return to the river. Salmon are strong swimmers and can move against fast water. People and animals both eat salmon. A salmon is not a shark.

[user]what is a sea creature?
[assistant]A sea creature is an animal that lives in the sea. Fish, crabs, jellyfish, sharks, and many others are sea creatures. Sea creatures have body parts that help them move and live in salt water. Some sea creatures stay near the shore, and some live far out in deeper water. A sea creature is not a land animal.

[user]what is a shark?
[assistant]A shark is a large fish with a strong body and sharp teeth. Sharks live in the sea and hunt other animals for food. Many sharks swim quickly and use a strong tail to move through the water. Some sharks are small, and some are very large. A shark is not a dolphin.

[user]what is a starfish?
[assistant]A starfish is a sea animal with arms spreading out from a center. Many starfish have five arms, but some have more. Starfish move slowly along rocks and the sea floor. Some starfish can grow back an arm after losing one. A starfish is not a fish.
