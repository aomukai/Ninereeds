[user]what is analogy?
[Ninereeds]An analogy is a way to compare two things that share a similarity. This comparison shows how one thing is like another thing to help explain an idea. For example, a brain compared to a computer is one kind of comparison that makes a new idea clearer. Another example is a teacher compared to a gardener. An analogy uses a familiar thing to make a new idea easier to understand. Analogy is not a fact.
