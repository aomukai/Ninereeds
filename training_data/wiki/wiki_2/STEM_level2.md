[user]what is atom?
[Ninereeds]An atom is the smallest unit of a material that keeps the properties of that material. Each atom has a central core called a nucleus with protons and neutrons. Tiny particles called electrons move around the nucleus. Those units join together to form every solid, liquid, and gas in the world. Water, for example, is made from particles of oxygen and hydrogen joined together. Atom is not a single object visible to the eye.

[user]what is a molecule?
[Ninereeds]A molecule is a tiny particle made of two or more atoms joined together. Different kinds of atoms join to form every substance in the world. Water has two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom in each particle. Carbon dioxide has one carbon atom and two oxygen atoms in each particle. Molecule is not a solid object.
