# Personal Identity And Self Description — Level 2

[user]what is self-knowledge?
[Ninereeds]Self-knowledge is a person's understanding of a person's own thoughts, feelings, and actions. A person learns about a person's own mind by paying close attention to a person's own choices and habits. For example, a person who knows that loud noises make a person feel scared has self-knowledge about that feeling. A person who understands that a person needs quiet time after a busy day also has this understanding. Self-knowledge is not guessing.
