# Levels Of Intensity And Gradation — Level 2

[user]what is a gradation?
[Ninereeds]A gradation is a series of small steps from one state to another along a scale. A sunset shows a series of colors moving from bright blue to warm orange and dark purple. Music can show a movement of sound from quiet to loud. Each step in a gradation is a small change from the state before. Gradation is not a sudden shift.
