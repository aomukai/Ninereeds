[user]what is a room?
[assistant]A room is a space inside a building enclosed by walls, a floor, and a ceiling. Each room in a house has its own purpose — some are for sleeping, some for cooking, and some for washing. You enter a room through a door, and the walls give you privacy from the rest of the building. A house is made up of several rooms that work together to make a home. A room is not a building, which is the whole structure that contains the rooms.

[user]what is a bathroom?
[assistant]A bathroom is a room where you wash yourself and use the toilet. It has a sink for washing hands and face, a toilet, and a bath or shower. People go to the bathroom to clean themselves, brush their teeth, and get ready for the day. The door of a bathroom usually has a lock so people have privacy. A bathroom is not a bedroom, which is used for sleeping.

[user]what is a bedroom?
[assistant]A bedroom is a room where a person sleeps and keeps their clothes and personal things. It has a bed, and often a wardrobe or shelves for storing belongings. Bedrooms are quiet and private, with a window that can be covered to block out light at night. Most people begin and end each day in their bedroom. A bedroom is not a bathroom, which is used for washing rather than sleeping.

[user]what is a kitchen?
[assistant]A kitchen is the room where food is prepared and cooked. It has a stove for cooking, a sink for washing, and a cold box for keeping food fresh. Pots, pans, and plates are stored in the kitchen, close to where they are needed. The kitchen is often where the smell of a meal first fills the rest of the house. A kitchen is not a dining room, which is where people sit down to eat the food the kitchen has prepared.

[user]what is a living room?
[assistant]A living room is the main room in a home where people relax and spend time together. It usually has soft chairs and a sofa, and many living rooms have a television. People sit in the living room to talk, read, or rest after a long day. It is also where guests are welcomed when they come to visit. A living room is not a bedroom, which is a private room used only for sleeping.
