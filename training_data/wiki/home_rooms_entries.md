[user]what is a bedroom?
[assistant]A bedroom is a room where a person sleeps. A bedroom usually has a bed and may have clothes, shelves, or other personal things. Bedrooms are often quiet and private. Many people begin and end the day in a bedroom. A bedroom is not a bathroom.

[user]what is a kitchen?
[assistant]A kitchen is the room where food is made and cooked. Kitchens often have a sink, a stove, and places to keep food and dishes. People wash, cut, mix, and cook in the kitchen. The kitchen is often full of good food smells. A kitchen is not a dining room.

[user]what is a dining room?
[assistant]A dining room is a room where people sit and eat together. A dining room often has a table and chairs. Families and guests may eat meals in a dining room. Some homes have a separate dining room and some do not. A dining room is not a kitchen.

[user]what is a living room?
