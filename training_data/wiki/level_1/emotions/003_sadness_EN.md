[user]what is sadness?
[Ninereeds]Sadness is a heavy emotion that makes you feel quiet, low, and unhappy. You might feel sadness when you lose something important, say goodbye to a friend, or feel hurt. When people are sad, they may cry, move slowly, and want to be alone. Sadness is a natural part of life that shows we care about what we have lost. Sadness is the opposite of happiness.
