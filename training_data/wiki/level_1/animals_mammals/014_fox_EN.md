[user]what is a fox?
[Ninereeds]A fox is a small, quick animal with a pointed nose, large ears, and a thick, bushy tail. Most foxes have bright reddish-orange fur. Foxes are clever hunters that eat mice, birds, and rabbits, and also eat berries and scraps of food they can find. They live alone and are most active at night. A fox is not a wolf, which is much larger and hunts together with a group.
