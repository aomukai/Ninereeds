# Home Objects Entries - Part 3 (Batch 8c)

[user]what is a key?
[assistant]A key is a small metal piece that opens locks. It has cuts and grooves that match a specific lock. Keys come for houses, cars, padlocks and cabinets. We use keys to open doors, start cars and open locked boxes. A key is not a password, which is a secret code for computers rather than a metal object.

[user]what is a lamp?
[assistant]A lamp is a device that makes light. It has a bulb or LED in a holder powered by electricity. Lamps help us see when reading, working or for room ambiance. They come as table lamps, floor lamps or hanging lights. We can change how bright or what color the light is. A lamp is not a flashlight, which you can hold in your hand and runs on batteries.

[user]what is pants?
[assistant]Pants are clothing that covers the legs. They go from the waist down to the ankles with two separate tubes. Pants come in styles like jeans, trousers or sweatpants. They are made from cotton, wool, polyester or denim. Pants usually have zippers, buttons or strings to close them. Pants are not a skirt, which is one tube of cloth around the waist.

[user]what is paper?
[assistant]Paper is a thin sheet made from wood or plant fibers. It is made by pressing wet fibers together and drying them. Paper is used for writing, printing and wrapping things. It comes in many thicknesses, textures and colors. We can recycle paper to make new paper. Paper is not plastic, which is made from oil and not from plants.

[user]what is a plate?
[assistant]A plate is a flat round dish for serving food. It is wider than it is deep and holds food in one layer. Plates come in many sizes from small to very large. They can be made of clay, glass, metal, wood or plastic. We use plates for meals, snacks and presenting food nicely. A plate is not a bowl, which is wider and deeper and can hold liquids.

[user]what is a shelf?
[assistant]A shelf is a flat horizontal surface for storing things. It attaches to a wall or fits inside a cabinet. Shelves keep items organized and easy to see or reach. They come in different lengths, depths and strengths. Shelves can be fixed in place or moved up and down. A shelf is not a drawer, which slides in and out for closed storage.

[user]what is a table?
[assistant]A table is furniture with a flat top and legs. It gives us a level surface to work, eat or put things on. Tables come in shapes like round, square, rectangle or oval. They are made of wood, metal, glass or plastic. We use tables for dinner, meetings, hobbies and games. A table is not a desk, which is mainly for focused work like homework or office tasks.

[user]what is a window?
[assistant]A window is an opening in a wall or roof. It is filled with glass or clear material to let light in. Windows help us see outside and get fresh air. They can be opened or closed as needed. Windows are made of glass, plastic, wood or metal in frames. A window is not a door, which is mainly for walking through rather than light.
