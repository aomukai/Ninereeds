# Tools and Kitchenware Entries (Batch 17)

[user]what is a brush?
[assistant]A brush is a tool with bristles attached to a handle. It is used for cleaning, grooming or painting. Brushes work by rubbing or soaking up materials. They come in types like hair, tooth or paint brushes. A brush is not a comb, which has rigid teeth instead of soft bristles.

[user]what is a button?
[assistant]A button is a small fastener for clothes. It is made of plastic, metal, wood or shell. Buttons go through holes in fabric to close it. They come in many shapes, sizes and colors. A button is not a zipper, which has interlocking teeth and a slider.

[user]what is a crayon?
[assistant]A crayon is a stick of colored wax. It is used for drawing or coloring. Crayons transfer color when pressed on paper. They are popular with children for being easy to use. A crayon is not a marker, which uses ink and a felt tip.

[user]what is a fork?
[assistant]A fork is an eating tool with a handle and prongs. It usually has two, three or four sharp tines. Forks are used to spear, lift and eat food. They are made of metal, plastic or wood. A fork is not a spoon, which has a bowl for scooping.

[user]what is a hammer?
[assistant]A hammer is a tool for hitting things. It has a heavy head on a handle. Hammers are used to drive nails or break objects. They come in types like claw, ball-peen or sledge hammers. A hammer is not a mallet, which has a soft head made of rubber or wood.

[user]what is a handsaw?
[assistant]A handsaw is a tool for cutting by hand. It has a blade with sharp teeth and a handle. Handsaws cut wood, plastic or metal with back-and-forth motion. They offer control but need more effort than power saws. A handsaw is not a chainsaw, which uses a motorized chain.

[user]what is a hook?
[assistant]A hook is a curved piece for catching or holding. It is usually made of metal or plastic. Hooks work by catching onto objects and using tension. They are used for fishing, hanging clothes or securing loads. A hook is not a clasp, which fastens two ends together.

[user]what is a knife?
[assistant]A knife is a tool with a sharp edge or blade. It often has a handle to hold it safely. Knives are used to cut, slice or chop food and materials. They can be made of metal, ceramic or other hard materials. A knife is not a spoon, which is for scooping rather than cutting.

[user]what is a lever?
[assistant]A lever is a simple machine for lifting. It is a beam that pivots on a fixed point called fulcrum. Levers trade off distance for force to move heavy things. They come in three types based on where the fulcrum is. A lever is not a pulley, which uses a wheel and rope to change force direction.

[user]what is a nail?
[assistant]A nail is a slender metal pin with a flat head. It is hammered into materials to join them. Nails come in many sizes and types like common or finishing nails. They hold by pushing material aside and friction. A nail is not a screw, which has twisted threads that turn to hold.

[user]what is a pan?
[assistant]A pan is a broad, shallow container for cooking. It has a handle and is made of metal, ceramic or glass. Pans conduct heat from stove to food for even cooking. They are used for frying, sautéing and baking. A pan is not a pot, which is deeper with higher sides.

[user]what is a skillet?
[assistant]A skillet is a frying pan with sloped sides. It has a long handle and is often made of cast iron. Skillets are used for stir-frying, sautéing and searing food. They can go from stove to oven for different cooking methods. A skillet is not a wok, which has deep curved sides and a round bottom.

[user]what is a spoon?
[assistant]A spoon is an eating tool with a handle and bowl. It is used to lift, transfer or eat food and liquid. Spoons come in sizes like tea, table or soup spoons. They work by scooping up materials to carry or consume. A spoon is not a fork, which has tines for spearing.

[user]what is a tool?
[assistant]A tool is an object that helps us do work. It extends what we can do with our hands alone. Tools range from simple knives to complex machines. They work by increasing force, speed or precision. Tools help us build, grow food and solve problems. A tool is not raw material, which is the substance something is made from.
