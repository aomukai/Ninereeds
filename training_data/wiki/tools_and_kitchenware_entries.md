# Tools and Kitchenware Entries

[user]what is a tool?
[assistant]A tool is an object that helps people do work. People use tools to build, fix, cut, carry, or shape things. Some tools are simple and small, and some are larger or heavier. A good tool makes a job easier to do. A tool is not furniture.

[user]what is kitchenware?
[assistant]Kitchenware means things people use in a kitchen. Pots, pans, spoons, forks, and knives are all kitchenware. Kitchenware helps people cook, serve, and eat food. Some kitchenware is made of metal, wood, glass, or plastic. Kitchenware is not furniture.

[user]what is a brush?
[assistant]A brush is a tool with bristles on one end. People use brushes for cleaning, painting, or grooming. A brush may be small enough for a hand or long enough to reach the floor. Brushes move dust, paint, or hair from one place to another. A brush is not a crayon.

[user]what is a fork?
[assistant]A fork is a piece of kitchenware used for eating. A fork has a handle and pointed parts called prongs. People use forks to pick up and hold food. Forks may be made of metal, wood, or plastic. A fork is not a spoon.

[user]what is a hammer?
[assistant]A hammer is a tool for hitting things. A hammer has a heavy head on a handle. People use hammers to drive nails into wood or other material. A hammer must be swung with care because it can hit hard. A hammer is not a handsaw.

[user]what is a handsaw?
[assistant]A handsaw is a tool for cutting by hand. A handsaw has a handle and a long blade with teeth. People move a handsaw back and forth to cut wood or other material. A handsaw works more slowly than a machine but gives good control. A handsaw is not a hammer.

[user]what is a hook?
[assistant]A hook is a curved piece used for holding or hanging things. A hook catches onto a loop, edge, or handle. People use hooks for hanging clothes, bags, or tools. Hooks may be made of metal or plastic. A hook is not a nail.

[user]what is a knife?
[assistant]A knife is a tool with a sharp blade. People use knives to cut food or other material. A knife usually has a handle for holding it safely. Knives must be used carefully because the blade is sharp. A knife is not a fork.

[user]what is a lever?
[assistant]A lever is a tool or simple machine used to lift or move things. A lever pushes on one side and moves around a firm point. People use levers to pry, lift, or press. A lever helps make a heavy job easier. A lever is not a hook.

[user]what is a nail?
[assistant]A nail is a small metal pin used for joining things. A nail usually has a sharp end and a flat head. People hit nails with a hammer to push them into wood or other material. Nails help hold parts together. A nail is not a hook.

[user]what is a pan?
[assistant]A pan is a piece of kitchenware used for cooking food. A pan often has a handle and a flat bottom. People use pans to fry, heat, or cook food on a stove. Pans may be made of metal or other strong material. A pan is not a plate.

[user]what is a skillet?
[assistant]A skillet is a kind of pan used for cooking. A skillet has a flat bottom, sloping sides, and a handle. People use skillets to fry or brown food. Some skillets are heavy and hold heat well. A skillet is not a pot.

[user]what is a spoon?
[assistant]A spoon is a piece of kitchenware used for eating or serving food. A spoon has a handle and a rounded bowl at one end. People use spoons for soup, cereal, or other soft food. Spoons can also be used to stir or scoop. A spoon is not a fork.
