# Material Composition Entries

[user]what is a material?
[assistant]A material is what something is made from. Wood, metal, plastic, glass, paper, and fabric are materials. People choose a material because of how it feels and what it can do. A material helps give an object its shape and use. A material is not a color.

[user]what does made of mean?
[assistant]Made of means built from one or more materials. A chair can be made of wood, and a bottle can be made of glass or plastic. People ask what something is made of when they want to know its material. The words made of help describe how an object is built. Made of is not a material.

[user]what is wood?
[assistant]Wood is a material that comes from trees. Wood can be strong, hard, and useful for building. People make tables, doors, pencils, and toys from wood. Wood can feel smooth or rough, depending on how it is shaped. Wood is not metal.

[user]what is metal?
[assistant]Metal is a strong material used for many tools and objects. Metal can be hard, shiny, and heavy. People use metal to make spoons, pans, bikes, and parts of machines. Many metals can be shaped when they are heated or worked on. Metal is not wood.

[user]what is plastic?
[assistant]Plastic is a material used to make many everyday things. Plastic can be light, smooth, and easy to shape. People use plastic for toys, bottles, containers, and parts of tools. Some plastic bends easily, and some plastic is firm. Plastic is not glass.

[user]what is glass?
[assistant]Glass is a hard clear material. People use glass for windows, jars, and some bottles. Glass lets light pass through it, so people can often see through it. Glass can break if it falls or gets hit hard. Glass is not plastic.

[user]what is paper?
[assistant]Paper is a thin material used for writing, drawing, and wrapping. Paper comes in sheets and can be folded, cut, or torn. People use paper for books, signs, notes, and art. Paper is lighter and thinner than wood because it is made in a different form. Paper is not fabric.

[user]what is fabric?
[assistant]Fabric is a material made from threads woven or joined together. People use fabric for clothes, blankets, curtains, and bags. Fabric can feel soft, rough, thick, or thin. Different fabrics work well for different jobs. Fabric is not paper.

[user]what is rubber?
[assistant]Rubber is a material that can stretch and bend. People use rubber for boots, tires, bands, and some handles. Rubber can help things grip, bounce, or stay dry. Rubber often feels springy or smooth. Rubber is not metal.
