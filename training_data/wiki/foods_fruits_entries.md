# Foods Fruits Entries

[user]what is a fruit?
[assistant]A fruit is a plant food that often tastes sweet or tart. Many fruits have seeds inside. Fruits grow on trees, bushes, vines, or other plants. People eat fruit as a snack, with meals, or in desserts. A fruit is not a vegetable.

[user]what is a berry?
[assistant]A berry is a small soft fruit. Many berries are red, blue, black, or purple. Berries can taste sweet or tart. People eat berries fresh or use them in jam and desserts. A berry is not a nut.

[user]what is an apple?
[assistant]An apple is a round fruit that grows on a tree. Apples can be red, green, or yellow. Apples are crisp and juicy inside. People eat apples raw, baked, or made into juice. An apple is not a pear.

[user]what is a banana?
[assistant]A banana is a long curved fruit with a peel. Bananas start green and often turn yellow when ripe. The fruit inside is soft and sweet. People eat bananas as a snack or cut them into other food. A banana is not a plantain.

[user]what is a blueberry?
[assistant]A blueberry is a small round blue fruit. Blueberries grow on bushes. They are sweet and easy to eat by the handful. People eat blueberries fresh or use them in muffins and pancakes. A blueberry is not a strawberry.

[user]what is a grape?
[assistant]A grape is a small round fruit that grows in clusters on a vine. Grapes can be green, red, or purple. Grapes are juicy and easy to eat. People eat grapes fresh or make them into juice and raisins. A grape is not a berry.

[user]what is an orange?
[assistant]An orange is a round citrus fruit with a thick peel. Inside, an orange has juicy pieces called segments. Oranges grow on trees in warm places. People eat oranges fresh or squeeze them into juice. An orange is not a lemon.

[user]what is a strawberry?
[assistant]A strawberry is a small red fruit with tiny seeds on the outside. Strawberries grow on low plants near the ground. They are sweet and juicy. People eat strawberries fresh or use them in cakes and desserts. A strawberry is not a blueberry.

[user]what is a watermelon?
[assistant]A watermelon is a large heavy fruit with a thick green rind. Inside, the fruit is juicy and often red. Watermelons grow on vines along the ground. People eat watermelon in slices, especially on hot days. A watermelon is not an orange.

[user]what is a cherry?
[assistant]A cherry is a small round fruit with a pit in the middle. Many cherries are red, though some are yellow. Cherries grow on trees and hang from stems. People eat cherries fresh or use them in pies and desserts. A cherry is not a grape.

[user]what is a peach?
[assistant]A peach is a round fruit with soft fuzzy skin. Inside, a peach is sweet and juicy and has a large pit. Peaches grow on trees. People eat peaches fresh or cook them in desserts. A peach is not a plum.

[user]what is a pear?
[assistant]A pear is a fruit that is wider at the bottom and narrower at the top. Pears can be green, yellow, or brown. The inside is soft, sweet, and a little grainy. People eat pears raw or cooked. A pear is not an apple.

[user]what is a mango?
[assistant]A mango is a sweet fruit that grows in warm places. A mango can be green, yellow, or red on the outside. Inside, it has soft orange flesh and a large flat pit. People eat mango fresh or use it in drinks and other foods. A mango is not a peach.

[user]what is a lemon?
[assistant]A lemon is a yellow citrus fruit. Lemons taste very sour. People use lemons in water, tea, cooking, and baking. The peel and juice are both useful. A lemon is not a lime.

[user]what is a plum?
[assistant]A plum is a round fruit with smooth skin. Plums can be purple, red, or yellow. Inside, a plum is soft and juicy and has one pit. People eat plums fresh or dried. A plum is not a cherry.

[user]what is a pineapple?
[assistant]A pineapple is a large fruit with rough skin and stiff leaves on top. Inside, a pineapple is yellow, juicy, and sweet. Pineapples grow on a plant close to the ground. People cut away the outside before eating the fruit inside. A pineapple is not a melon.

[user]what is a nut?
[assistant]A nut is a hard dry seed with a shell. Nuts grow on trees or bushes. People eat nuts as snacks or use them in cooking and baking. Many animals gather nuts for food. A nut is not a berry.

[user]what is a peanut?
[assistant]A peanut is a small seed that grows in a shell underground. People eat peanuts roasted or made into peanut butter. Even though the name says nut, a peanut is not a true nut. Peanuts grow more like beans. A peanut is not a berry.

[user]what is a walnut?
[assistant]A walnut is a round nut with a very hard shell. Inside is a pale wrinkled part that people eat. Walnuts grow on trees. People eat walnuts on their own or use them in bread and cakes. A walnut is not an almond.

[user]what is an almond?
[assistant]An almond is a long oval nut. Almonds grow on trees in warm dry places. People eat almonds raw or roasted. Almonds can also be made into flour or milk. An almond is not a walnut.

[user]what is a chestnut?
[assistant]A chestnut is a shiny brown nut that grows inside a spiky case. Chestnuts are softer than many other nuts. People often roast chestnuts before eating them. Chestnuts have a sweet earthy taste. A chestnut is not a walnut.

[user]what is a plantain?
[assistant]A plantain is a fruit that looks like a large banana. Plantains are usually cooked before eating because they are starchy and less sweet than a banana. People fry, bake, or boil plantains in many meals. Plantains are popular in warm parts of the world. A plantain is not a banana.

[user]what is a lime?
[assistant]A lime is a small round citrus fruit with green skin. Limes taste very sour, like a lemon but with a different flavor. People squeeze lime juice into drinks, cooking, and salsa. Limes grow on trees in warm places. A lime is not a lemon.

[user]what is a melon?
[assistant]A melon is a large round or oval fruit with a thick rind. Inside, the flesh is soft, sweet, and juicy. Melons grow on vines along the ground. People eat melon sliced or in chunks as a snack or dessert. A melon is not a watermelon, though both have thick rinds and juicy insides.
