# Body Parts Entries

[user]what is a body part?
[assistant]A body part is one piece of a body. The head, arm, leg, hand, and foot are all body parts. Each body part has its own job. Body parts work together so a body can move, feel, and stay alive. A body part is not the whole body.

[user]what is a head?
[assistant]A head is the top part of the body. The head holds the brain, the face, the eyes, the ears, and the mouth. The head can turn from side to side and nod up and down. People and many animals have a head. A head is not a face.

[user]what is a face?
[assistant]A face is the front part of the head. The face holds the eyes, nose, and mouth. People know each other by their faces. Faces can smile, frown, and show feelings. A face is not a head.

[user]what is an arm?
[assistant]An arm is the body part between the shoulder and the hand. People use arms to reach, lift, carry, and hold things. Each arm has an elbow in the middle. People have two arms. An arm is not a leg.

[user]what is a leg?
[assistant]A leg is the body part between the hip and the foot. Legs help the body stand, walk, run, jump, and kick. Each leg has a knee in the middle. People have two legs. A leg is not an arm.

[user]what is a hand?
[assistant]A hand is the body part at the end of an arm. Each hand has fingers and a thumb. Hands are used for holding, touching, writing, eating, and waving. The inside of a hand is called the palm. A hand is not a foot.

[user]what is a foot?
[assistant]A foot is the body part at the end of a leg. Feet touch the ground when people stand or walk. Feet help the body balance and move. People have two feet. A foot is not a hand.

[user]what is an ear?
[assistant]An ear is the body part used for hearing. People have two ears, one on each side of the head. Ears help people hear sounds around them. Ears also help the body keep balance. An ear is not an earlobe.

[user]what is an earlobe?
[assistant]An earlobe is the soft bottom part of an ear. It hangs below the harder part of the ear. Some people wear earrings in the earlobe. The earlobe is part of the ear. An earlobe is not the whole ear.

[user]what is an eye?
[assistant]An eye is the body part used for seeing. Light goes into the eye so the brain can help make a picture of the world. People have two eyes. Eyes can be different colors. An eye is not an eyelid.

[user]what is an eyelid?
[assistant]An eyelid is the thin skin that opens and closes over an eye. Eyelids help protect the eyes. Eyelids close when people blink or sleep. Eyelids help keep the eyes moist. An eyelid is not an eyebrow.

[user]what is a mouth?
[assistant]A mouth is the opening in the face used for eating, drinking, and speaking. The mouth holds the teeth and the tongue. Food goes into the body through the mouth. The mouth also helps shape words and songs. A mouth is not a nose.

[user]what is a nose?
[assistant]A nose is the body part used for smelling and breathing. Air goes in and out through the nose. The nose can notice many smells. When a nose is blocked, breathing can feel harder. A nose is not a mouth.

[user]what is a belly?
[assistant]A belly is the soft front part of the body below the chest. Food goes into the belly area after eating. A belly can feel full after a meal. A hungry belly may rumble. A belly is not a chest.

[user]what is a backbone?
[assistant]A backbone is the long line of small bones in the middle of the back. The backbone helps hold the body upright. It also helps the body bend and turn. The backbone is very important for the body. A backbone is not a rib.

[user]what is a collarbone?
[assistant]A collarbone is the thin bone near the top of the chest. It helps connect the shoulder to the rest of the body. You can often feel a collarbone under the skin. People have a collarbone on each side. A collarbone is not a shoulder blade.

[user]what is a fingertip?
[assistant]A fingertip is the very end of a finger. Fingertips are very sensitive and can feel small things. People use fingertips for careful tasks. Fingertips help with touching and picking up tiny things. A fingertip is not a knuckle.

[user]what is a forehead?
[assistant]A forehead is the flat part of the face above the eyes. It sits below the hair and above the eyebrows. People may touch a forehead to check for fever. A forehead is part of the face. A forehead is not a cheek.

[user]what is a jawbone?
[assistant]A jawbone is the bone at the lower part of the mouth. The jawbone moves when people eat, talk, or yawn. The lower teeth sit in the jawbone. You can feel the jawbone under the face. A jawbone is not a tooth.

[user]what is a shinbone?
[assistant]A shinbone is the hard bone at the front of the lower leg. It runs from below the knee toward the ankle. The shinbone helps hold the body up. Bumping the shinbone can hurt a lot. A shinbone is not a kneecap.
