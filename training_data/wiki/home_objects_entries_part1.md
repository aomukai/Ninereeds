# Home Objects Entries - Part 1 (Batch 8a)

[user]what is a bed?
[assistant]A bed is furniture for sleeping or resting. It has a frame and a mattress to lie on. Beds come in sizes like twin, full, queen and king. They can be made of wood, metal or cloth-covered materials. Many beds have headboards and footboards for style. A bed is not a couch, which is made for sitting rather than sleeping.

[user]what is a book?
[assistant]A book is pages with words glued or sewn together. It has a front cover, back cover and spine. Books contain stories, facts or ideas to read and share. They can be hardcover, paperback or digital. Books come in many lengths from short to very long. A book is not a magazine, which comes out regularly and is thinner.

[user]what is a bookshelf?
[assistant]A bookshelf is furniture with flat shelves for books. It can stand alone or be built into a wall. Bookshelves hold books upright and organized. They are made of wood, metal or plastic. Bookshelves keep books safe and easy to find. A bookshelf is not a cabinet, which usually has doors for closed storage.

[user]what is a bowl?
[assistant]A bowl is a round open container for food or things. It is wider than it is tall. Bowls come in many sizes from tiny to large. They can be made of clay, glass, metal, wood or plastic. We use bowls for soup, cereal, salad and mixing food. A bowl is not a plate, which is flat and holds food in one layer.

[user]what is a box?
[assistant]A box is a container with flat sides and a lid. It comes in many sizes from small to very big. Boxes can be made of cardboard, wood, metal or plastic. They keep things safe from dust, water and damage. Boxes can be labeled, stacked or taped shut. A box is not a bag, which is soft and usually has handles.

[user]what is a broom?
[assistant]A broom is a tool for sweeping floors clean. It has stiff fibers attached to a long handle. Brooms gather dust and dirt into a pile. They work on wood, tile, concrete and outdoor floors. Brooms are simple, cheap and need no electricity. A broom is not a vacuum cleaner, which uses suction to pull up dirt.

[user]what is a bucket?
[assistant]A bucket is a round container with a handle. It carries liquids, solids or other things. Buckets are made of metal, plastic or wood. They come in sizes from small to very large. We use buckets for cleaning, gardening and building work. A bucket is not a bowl, which is wider and usually has no handle.

[user]what is a chair?
[assistant]A chair is furniture for one person to sit on. It has a back and usually four legs. Chairs let us rest, work, eat or talk while sitting up. They come in many styles for different uses. Chairs can be made of wood, metal, plastic or cloth. A chair is not a bench, which is made for many people to sit together.
