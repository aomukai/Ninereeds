# Colors Entries

[user]what is a color?
[assistant]A color is what something looks like when light shines on it. Red, blue, green, and yellow are colors. Colors help people describe and notice the world around them. Without light, colors are hard to see. A color is not a shape.

[user]what is light?
[assistant]Light is what helps people see. Light comes from the sun, lamps, fire, and other bright things. Without light, colors are hard to see. Light can be bright or soft. Light is not dark.

[user]what is dark?
[assistant]Dark means there is very little light. In the dark, colors are hard to see. Night is often dark when there is no bright light nearby. Some animals can move well in the dark. Dark is not light.

[user]what is red?
[assistant]Red is a strong bright color. Fire, blood, and many apples are red. Red is easy to notice and stands out. Many signs use red to warn people. Red is not blue.

[user]what is blue?
[assistant]Blue is the color of the sky on a clear day and of deep water. Many flowers, birds, and butterflies can be blue. Blue is often seen as a calm color. Blue can be light or dark. Blue is not yellow.

[user]what is yellow?
[assistant]Yellow is a bright light color. The sun, lemons, and many flowers can look yellow. Yellow is easy to notice from far away. Many people think of yellow as a warm color. Yellow is not red.

[user]what is green?
[assistant]Green is the color of grass, leaves, and many plants. Green is very common in nature. Some green things are bright, and some are dark. Green often makes people think of growing things. Green is not orange.

[user]what is orange?
[assistant]Orange is a bright warm color. Oranges, carrots, and pumpkins can be orange. Fire and sunset can also look orange. Orange stands out and is easy to notice. Orange is not purple.

[user]what is purple?
[assistant]Purple is a strong color between red and blue. Grapes, plums, and some flowers can be purple. Some purple is dark, and some is light. Purple is less common in nature than green or brown. Purple is not green.

[user]what is black?
[assistant]Black is the darkest color. The night sky, some animals, and some ink can look black. Black can make other colors stand out more. Many shadows look black or almost black. Black is the opposite of white.

[user]what is white?
[assistant]White is the lightest color. Snow, milk, and many clouds look white. White is bright and easy to see in the dark. Paper and walls are often white. White is the opposite of black.

[user]what is brown?
[assistant]Brown is the color of soil, tree bark, and many animals. Bread, wood, and chocolate can also be brown. Brown is a common color in nature. Brown often looks warm and earthy. Brown is not pink.

[user]what is pink?
[assistant]Pink is a light color close to red. Many flowers, some birds, and some clothes are pink. Pink can be soft and pale or bright and strong. Pink is easy to notice against green leaves or blue sky. Pink is not brown.

[user]what is gray?
[assistant]Gray is a color between black and white. Many stones, clouds, and metal things look gray. Gray can be light or dark. Gray is often a quiet-looking color. Gray is not bright white.

[user]what is gold?
[assistant]Gold is a bright shiny yellow color. Coins, crowns, and some leaves can look gold. Gold often shines in light. Gold is richer and deeper than plain yellow. Gold is not yellow.

[user]what is silver?
[assistant]Silver is a shiny light gray color. Spoons, coins, and many metal things can look silver. Silver reflects light and often looks bright. Silver is not the same as flat gray. Silver is not gray.

[user]what is clear?
[assistant]Clear means light can pass through something. Clean water, air, and glass can be clear. When something is clear, you can see through it. Clear things may have little color or no color. Clear is the opposite of opaque.

[user]what is opaque?
[assistant]Opaque means light cannot pass through something. Wood, stone, and walls are opaque. You cannot see through an opaque thing. Many everyday objects are opaque. Opaque is the opposite of clear.

[user]what is a rainbow?
[assistant]A rainbow is a curved band of colors in the sky. A rainbow can appear when sunlight shines through rain. A rainbow shows several colors together. Many children notice red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple in a rainbow. A rainbow is not a cloud.

[user]what is the spectrum?
[assistant]The spectrum is the full range of colors in light. A rainbow shows part of the spectrum. The spectrum helps people think about colors as one big set. Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple are all part of the spectrum. The spectrum is not a single color.
