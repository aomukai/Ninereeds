# Smells and Tastes Entries

[user]what is a smell?
[assistant]A smell is something noticed with the nose. Bread, flowers, smoke, and soap can each have a smell. Smells can be strong or weak. People use smell to learn about food, places, and nearby things. A smell is not a taste.

[user]what does stinky mean?
[assistant]Stinky means having a strong bad smell. Trash, dirty socks, or rotten food can smell stinky. A stinky smell may make people wrinkle the nose or move away. The word is common in everyday child talk. Stinky is not fresh.

[user]what does fresh mean?
[assistant]Fresh can mean clean and pleasant in smell or newly made and not old. Fresh air and fresh bread can smell good. The word often points to something clean, new, or full of life. Fresh can be used for food, air, and flowers. Fresh is not stinky.

[user]what does yummy mean?
[assistant]Yummy means tasting or smelling very good in a pleasant way. A child may call cake, fruit, or soup yummy. The word is common in casual child speech. Yummy shows enjoyment of food. Yummy is not yucky.

[user]what does yucky mean?
[assistant]Yucky means tasting, smelling, or feeling unpleasant. A child may call rotten food or a bad smell yucky. The word is informal and common in everyday speech. Yucky shows dislike. Yucky is not yummy.

[user]what does delicious mean?
[assistant]Delicious means tasting very good. A delicious meal or snack is pleasing and enjoyable to eat. People often use delicious for food more than for smells alone. The word is a stronger and more grown-up word than yummy. Delicious is not bitter.

[user]what does salty mean?
[assistant]Salty means having the taste of salt. Chips, soup, and sea water can taste salty. A little salt can change the flavor of food a lot. Salty is one common kind of taste. Salty is not sweet.

[user]what does bitter mean?
[assistant]Bitter is a sharp taste that is not sweet. Some dark greens and some medicines can taste bitter. Bitter is a different kind of taste from sour or salty. People do not all like bitter foods in the same way. Bitter is not delicious.

[user]what does spicy mean?
[assistant]Spicy means having a hot strong flavor in the mouth. Some soups, sauces, and snacks can taste spicy. Spicy food may make a person want water. Spicy is a flavor feeling many children notice quickly. Spicy is not bland.

[user]what does it smells like mean?
[assistant]It smells like means the speaker is comparing one smell to another known smell. A child may say it smells like oranges or it smells like smoke. The phrase helps describe a smell by using something familiar. People use it when the smell is hard to name directly. It smells like is not it tastes like.

[user]what does it tastes like mean?
[assistant]It tastes like means the speaker is comparing one taste to another known taste. A child may say it tastes like apple or it tastes like lemon. The phrase helps describe a flavor through comparison. People use it when the taste is easier to explain by naming something similar. It tastes like is not it smells like.
