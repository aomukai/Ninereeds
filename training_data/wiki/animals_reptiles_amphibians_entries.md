# Animals Reptiles and Amphibians Entries

[user]what is a reptile?
[assistant]A reptile is an animal with dry skin covered in scales or hard plates. Many reptiles lay eggs, and many like warm places. Lizards, snakes, turtles, crocodiles, and alligators are reptiles. Reptiles do not have feathers or fur. A reptile is not a bird.

[user]what is an amphibian?
[assistant]An amphibian is an animal that often lives in water when young and on land when older. Amphibians have soft skin, not dry scales. Frogs and toads are amphibians. Many amphibians begin life in water and change as they grow. An amphibian is not a reptile.

[user]what is an alligator?
[assistant]An alligator is a large reptile that lives in fresh water. It has a wide snout, a strong tail, and tough skin on its back. Alligators wait quietly and catch fish, birds, and other animals. They live in swamps, rivers, and other wet places. An alligator is not a crocodile.

[user]what is a crocodile?
[assistant]A crocodile is a large reptile with a long, narrow snout. Crocodiles live in warm wet places and are strong swimmers. Some crocodiles live in fresh water, and some live near salt water. Crocodiles wait for animals to come close and then catch them. A crocodile is not an alligator.

[user]what is a frog?
[assistant]A frog is a small amphibian with smooth, wet skin. Frogs have long back legs that help them jump and swim. Many frogs start life in water and change as they grow. Frogs eat insects and other very small animals. A frog is not a toad.

[user]what is a lizard?
[assistant]A lizard is a reptile with a long body and usually four legs. Lizards have dry, scaly skin and like warm places. Some lizards are tiny, and some are much larger. Many lizards eat insects and other small animals. A lizard is not a snake.

[user]what is a snake?
[assistant]A snake is a long reptile with no legs. Snakes move by bending and pushing their bodies along the ground. Many snakes use their tongue to sense the air around them. Snakes eat animals they can swallow whole. A snake is not a worm.

[user]what is a toad?
[assistant]A toad is an amphibian that looks a lot like a frog. A toad has drier, bumpier skin and shorter legs than a frog. Toads often walk or make short hops instead of long jumps. They eat insects and other small animals. A toad is not a frog.

[user]what is a turtle?
[assistant]A turtle is a reptile with a hard shell. The shell helps protect the turtle's body. Some turtles live in water, some live on land, and some live in both places. Turtles move slowly and can live for many years. A turtle is not a tortoise.
