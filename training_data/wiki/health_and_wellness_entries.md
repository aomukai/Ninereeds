# Health and Wellness Entries

[user]what is health?
[assistant]Health is the state of the body and mind when they are working well. Good health helps a person play, learn, move, and rest. Food, water, sleep, cleaning, and care all help health. Health is not the same as sickness.

[user]what is wellness?
[assistant]Wellness means taking care of the body and mind in steady ways. Wellness includes food, rest, movement, and clean habits. Wellness helps a person stay healthy over time. Wellness is not the same as illness.

[user]what is a fever?
[assistant]A fever is when the body gets hotter than usual during sickness. A person with a fever may feel hot, tired, or weak. A fever can be a sign that the body is fighting illness. A fever is not the same as health.

[user]what is a cough?
[assistant]A cough is a sudden push of air out of the throat and mouth. People cough when the throat or chest feels bothered. A cough can happen during a cold or another sickness. A cough is not the same as quiet breathing.

[user]what is a sore throat?
[assistant]A sore throat is pain or discomfort in the throat. A person with a sore throat may not want to swallow or talk much. A sore throat can happen during sickness. A sore throat is not the same as comfort.

[user]what is a headache?
[assistant]A headache is pain in the head. A headache may make it hard to think, play, or rest well. People with a headache may want quiet, water, or sleep. A headache is not the same as comfort.

[user]what is a stomachache?
[assistant]A stomachache is pain or discomfort in the belly. A person with a stomachache may want to sit still, curl up, or rest. A stomachache can happen after eating, during sickness, or for other reasons. A stomachache is not the same as hunger.

[user]what is a cut?
[assistant]A cut is an opening in the skin made by something sharp or rough. A cut may bleed and may need cleaning and care. Small cuts can happen during play or work. A cut is not the same as healthy skin.

[user]what is a bruise?
[assistant]A bruise is a sore mark on the body after a bump or hit. A bruise may look blue, purple, or dark. Bruises can hurt even when the skin is not open. A bruise is not the same as clear skin.

[user]what is a bandage?
[assistant]A bandage is something used to cover and protect an injured place on the body. People put a bandage on a cut, scrape, or sore spot. A bandage helps keep the place clean while it heals. A bandage is not the same as a sticker.

[user]what is medicine?
[assistant]Medicine is something used to help treat sickness, pain, or another health problem. Some medicine is a liquid, some is a pill, and some is a cream. Medicine should be given in a careful and safe way. Medicine is not candy.

[user]what is a germ?
[assistant]A germ is a very tiny living thing that can sometimes make people sick. Germs can spread through touch, coughs, or dirty surfaces. Washing hands can help stop some germs from spreading. A germ is not something a person can usually see.
