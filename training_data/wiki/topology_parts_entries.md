# Topology Parts Entries (Batch 18)

[user]what is a bottle?
[assistant]A bottle is a narrow container for holding liquids. It has a body that holds the content and a neck that can be closed. Bottles are made of glass, plastic or metal that does not let liquid through. They come in many sizes from tiny vials to large carboys. A bottle is not a jar, which usually has a wider opening and is often used for solid foods.

[user]what is a branch?
[assistant]A branch is a woody part growing from a tree trunk. It grows out from the trunk and holds leaves, flowers and fruit. Branches are divided into smaller branches and twigs. They move water from roots to leaves and sugar from leaves to the tree. A branch is not a root, which grows underground to get water and hold the tree.

[user]what is a cup?
[assistant]A cup is a small container for holding drinks. It has an open top and usually a handle. Cups hold one serving of coffee, tea or water. They are made to fit comfortably in one hand. Cups come in sizes from tiny to large. They can be plain or decorated with pictures. A cup is not a glass, which is taller and usually has no handle.

[user]what is a hilltop?
[assistant]A hilltop is the highest point of a hill. A hill is raised land that is lower than a mountain. From a hilltop you can often see the land around for miles. Hilltops are often windier and cooler than lower areas. People may go to hilltops for the view or to build things. A hilltop is not the bottom of a valley, which is the lowest point between hills or mountains and often has water and dirt.

[user]what is a river?
[assistant]A river is a large natural flow of water in a channel. It starts from rain, snow melt or springs and flows downhill. Rivers can be wide and deep enough for boats to travel on. They give water for drinking, farming and making electricity. A river is not a canal, which is a human-made waterway often built straight.

[user]what is a road?
[assistant]A road is a wide path for vehicles to travel from place to place. It has a prepared surface for cars, trucks and bikes to move safely. Roads can be covered with asphalt, concrete, gravel or dirt. They connect cities, towns and countryside areas. A road is not a sidewalk, which is a path for people to walk on beside the road.

[user]what is a tree?
[assistant]A tree is a tall perennial plant with a woody trunk. It has branches and leaves growing from its trunk. Trees are defined by their height and wood structure. They live for many years, sometimes hundreds. Trees make oxygen, give homes and hold soil in place. A tree is not a shrub, which is shorter with many stems.
