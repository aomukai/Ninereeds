# Mathematical Concepts Entries

[user]what is a number?
[assistant]A number is a word or symbol used for counting and measuring. Numbers help people say how many things there are. People use numbers every day for time, counting, and other tasks. Some numbers are small and some are very large. A number is not a letter.

[user]what is zero?
[assistant]Zero is a number that means there is nothing to count. It is written as the symbol 0. Zero comes before one when you count. If you eat all the apples in a bowl, the bowl has zero apples left. Zero is not the same as one.

[user]what is one?
[assistant]One is a number. We write it with the symbol 1. One means a single thing, not two and not zero. If you have one apple, there is just that apple and nothing else. One is not two.

[user]what is two?
[assistant]Two is a number. We write it with the symbol 2. Two means one thing and then one more. A pair of shoes is two shoes. Two is not one, and two is not three.

[user]what is three?
[assistant]Three is a number. We write it with the symbol 3. Three comes after two when you count. A triangle has three sides and three corners. Three is not two, and three is not four.

[user]what is four?
[assistant]Four is a number. We write it with the symbol 4. Four comes after three when you count. A square has four sides and four corners. Four is not three, and four is not five.

[user]what is five?
[assistant]Five is a number. We write it with the symbol 5. Five comes after four when you count. A hand has five fingers. Five is not four, and five is not six.

[user]what is six?
[assistant]Six is a number. We write it with the symbol 6. Six comes after five when you count. Six is two groups of three. Six is not five, and six is not seven.

[user]what is seven?
[assistant]Seven is a number. We write it with the symbol 7. Seven comes after six when you count. A week has seven days. Seven is not six, and seven is not eight.

[user]what is eight?
[assistant]Eight is a number. We write it with the symbol 8. Eight comes after seven when you count. Eight is two groups of four. Eight is not seven, and eight is not nine.

[user]what is nine?
[assistant]Nine is a number. We write it with the symbol 9. Nine comes after eight when you count. Nine is three groups of three. Nine is not eight, and nine is not ten.

[user]what is ten?
[assistant]Ten is a number. We write it with the symbol 10. Ten comes after nine when you count. Two hands together have ten fingers. Ten is not nine, and ten is not eleven.

[user]what is counting?
[assistant]Counting means saying numbers in order to find how many things there are. People count by saying one, two, three, and so on. Counting helps people keep track of objects. You can count apples, chairs, or people. Counting is not guessing.

[user]what is addition?
[assistant]Addition means putting numbers together. When you add two and three, you get five. Addition makes a larger total. Addition uses the plus sign. Addition is not subtraction.

[user]what is subtraction?
[assistant]Subtraction means taking one number away from another. When you subtract two from five, you get three. Subtraction makes the amount smaller. Subtraction uses the minus sign. Subtraction is not addition.

[user]what does "plus" mean?
[assistant]Plus is the word and sign (+) used for adding. When people see plus, they know numbers are being put together. Plus means the total will get bigger. For example, 2 plus 2 is 4. Plus is the opposite of minus.

[user]what does "minus" mean?
[assistant]Minus is the word and sign (-) used for taking away. When people see minus, they know the amount will get smaller. Minus shows that one part is being removed from another amount. For example, 5 minus 2 is 3. Minus is the opposite of plus.

[user]what does "equals" mean?
[assistant]Equals is the word and sign (=) that show two amounts are the same. When people say 2 plus 2 equals 4, both sides match. Equals means exactly the same amount, not almost. Equals helps show a correct math sentence. Equals means both sides are equal.

[user]what is equal?
[assistant]Equal means the same in amount, size, or value. Two groups are equal if they have the same number of things. Four plus one is equal to five. Equal means matching, not more and not less. Equal is not more than or less than.

[user]what is more than?
[assistant]More than means a greater number or amount. If there are five birds and three cats, there are more birds than cats. People use more than to compare two groups. More means a bigger amount. More than is not less than.

[user]what is less than?
[assistant]Less than means a smaller number or amount. If there are two dogs and six fish, there are fewer dogs than fish. People use less than to compare two groups. Less means a smaller amount. Less than is the opposite of more than.

[user]what is a half?
[assistant]A half is one of two equal parts. If you cut an apple into two equal pieces, each piece is a half. Two halves make one whole. Half of four is two. A half is not a whole.

[user]what is a shape?
[assistant]A shape is the form or outline of something. Balls, boxes, and signs all have shapes. Shapes can be drawn on paper or seen in the world. Shape helps people notice and describe how something looks. A shape is not a color.

[user]what is a circle?
[assistant]A circle is a round flat shape with no corners. Coins, wheels, and clocks can be circles. A circle is smooth all around the edge. A circle looks round from every side. A circle is not a square.

[user]what does it mean for something to be "round"?
[assistant]Round means shaped like a circle or curved all around. A ball, a coin, and the full moon can look round. Round things do not have sharp corners. Round is a word people use for both flat shapes and solid things. Round is not the same as square.

[user]what is a square?
[assistant]A square is a flat shape with four equal sides and four corners. Tiles, windows, and some boxes are square-shaped. All four sides of a square are the same length. A square has straight edges, not a round edge. A square is not a circle.

[user]what is a triangle?
[assistant]A triangle is a flat shape with three sides and three corners. Triangles can be big or small, but they always have three sides. Rooftops and slices of pizza can look like triangles. A triangle has fewer sides than a square. A triangle is not a square.

[user]what is a rectangle?
[assistant]A rectangle is a flat shape with four sides and four corners. Doors, books, and screens are often rectangles. The opposite sides of a rectangle match in length. A rectangle is like a square, but not all sides have to be the same. A rectangle is not a triangle.
