# Foods and Drinks Entries

[user]what is food?
[assistant]Food is what living things eat for energy and growth. Food can come from plants or from animals. People eat food at meals and also as snacks. Food helps the body stay strong and healthy. Food is not a drink.

[user]what is a drink?
[assistant]A drink is a liquid that people or animals can swallow. Water, milk, and juice are drinks. Some drinks are cold, and some drinks are warm. A drink helps with thirst and may also go with a meal. A drink is not food.

[user]what is bread?
[assistant]Bread is a food made from flour and water. Bread is baked in an oven until the outside is firm and the inside is soft. People eat bread plain, toasted, or with other food. Bread can be cut into slices for meals or snacks. Bread is not rice.

[user]what is cheese?
[assistant]Cheese is a food made from milk. Cheese can be soft or hard and may be white, yellow, or orange. People eat cheese in slices, in pieces, or melted on hot food. Cheese has a rich taste and is often kept cool until it is eaten. Cheese is not milk.

[user]what is a cookie?
[assistant]A cookie is a small sweet baked food. Cookies can be soft or crisp. Some cookies have chocolate, nuts, or sugar on top. People often eat cookies as a snack or a treat. A cookie is not bread.

[user]what is a cupcake?
[assistant]A cupcake is a small cake made for one person. A cupcake is baked in a paper cup or small pan. Many cupcakes have sweet icing on top. People often eat cupcakes at parties or on birthdays. A cupcake is not bread.

[user]what is an egg?
[assistant]An egg is a food with a shell around it. Many people cook and eat eggs from chickens. Inside an egg are the white part and the yellow part called the yolk. Eggs can be boiled, fried, or mixed into other food. An egg is not milk.

[user]what is honey?
[assistant]Honey is a sweet thick food made by bees. Honey comes from flower nectar that bees collect. Honey can be eaten on bread or mixed into other food. It is sticky and golden or brown in color. Honey is not milk.

[user]what is milk?
[assistant]Milk is a white drink that comes from animals such as cows or goats. Many children drink milk with meals or with breakfast. Milk can be used in cooking, baking, or poured over cereal. Milk is kept cold so it stays fresh. Milk is not water.

[user]what is a milkshake?
[assistant]A milkshake is a cold sweet drink made with milk and other sweet things. Many milkshakes are thick and smooth. People often drink milkshakes through a straw. Milkshakes can taste like chocolate, vanilla, or strawberry. A milkshake is not milk.

[user]what is popcorn?
[assistant]Popcorn is a light crunchy food made from corn. Hard corn kernels pop open when they get hot. Popcorn can be plain, salty, or buttery. People often eat popcorn as a snack while watching a film or resting. Popcorn is not bread.

[user]what is rice?
[assistant]Rice is a small grain that people cook and eat. Cooked rice becomes soft and is often served in a bowl or on a plate. Rice can go with vegetables, meat, or sauce. Many people around the world eat rice often. Rice is not bread.

[user]what is soup?
[assistant]Soup is a warm liquid food. Soup is eaten from a bowl, often with a spoon. Soup may have vegetables, meat, noodles, or other small pieces in it. Many people like soup when the weather is cold or when they do not feel well. Soup is not a drink.

[user]what is water?
[assistant]Water is a clear drink that living things need every day. People drink water when they are thirsty. Water is also used for washing, cooking, and growing food. Rivers, lakes, rain, and ice are all forms of water. Water is not milk.
