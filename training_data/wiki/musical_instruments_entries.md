# Musical Instruments Entries

[user]what is a musical instrument?
[assistant]A musical instrument is something people use to make music. Musical instruments can be hit, blown, shaken, or played with fingers. Children see musical instruments at school, at home, and in bands. A musical instrument helps make sound in a controlled way. A musical instrument is not a craft.

[user]what is a guitar?
[assistant]A guitar is a musical instrument with strings. People hold a guitar and make sound by plucking or strumming the strings. Guitars can be small or large and often have a wooden body. A guitar can play melody or rhythm in music. A guitar is not a drum.

[user]what is a piano?
[assistant]A piano is a musical instrument with keys. People press the keys to make different notes. A piano can play low sounds and high sounds in the same instrument. Many pianos are large, but some are smaller keyboards. A piano is not a flute.

[user]what is a drum?
[assistant]A drum is a musical instrument that people hit to make sound. Many drums have a tight top surface that vibrates when struck. Drums can make steady beats for songs and group music. A drum is part of many music styles around the world. A drum is not a violin.

[user]what is a violin?
[assistant]A violin is a small musical instrument with strings. People hold a violin under the chin and play it with a bow or with fingers. Violins can make high clear sounds. A violin is often used in groups of string instruments. A violin is not a drum.

[user]what is a flute?
[assistant]A flute is a musical instrument people play by blowing air. A flute is long and narrow and has holes or keys. The sound changes when fingers cover or uncover parts of the flute. A flute can make light clear notes. A flute is not a trumpet.

[user]what is a trumpet?
[assistant]A trumpet is a musical instrument people play by blowing air into it. A trumpet is made from metal and has buttons called valves. Trumpets can make bright strong sounds. A trumpet is common in bands and music groups. A trumpet is not a flute.

[user]what is a recorder?
[assistant]A recorder is a simple wind instrument children often play at school. People make sound in a recorder by blowing gently and covering holes with fingers. A recorder is smaller and easier for beginners than many other instruments. A recorder can play clear notes one by one. A recorder is not a drum.

[user]what is a xylophone?
[assistant]A xylophone is a musical instrument with bars in a row. People hit the bars with small beaters to make notes. Different bars make different pitches because they are different sizes. A xylophone lets children see and hear notes in order. A xylophone is not a piano.

[user]what is a string instrument?
[assistant]A string instrument is a musical instrument that makes sound with strings. A guitar and a violin are string instruments. People play string instruments by plucking, strumming, or using a bow. The strings vibrate to make musical notes. A string instrument is not a wind instrument.

[user]what is a wind instrument?
[assistant]A wind instrument is a musical instrument people play by blowing air. A flute, recorder, and trumpet are wind instruments. Air moving through the instrument helps make the sound. Fingers or buttons help change the notes. A wind instrument is not a string instrument.

[user]what is percussion?
[assistant]Percussion is music made by hitting, shaking, or tapping instruments. Drums, cymbals, and shakers are percussion instruments. Percussion often helps keep the beat in music. Many percussion sounds are short, clear, and strong. Percussion is not a wind instrument.

[user]what does play an instrument mean?
[assistant]Play an instrument means use a musical instrument to make music. A child may play an instrument alone or with a group. Playing an instrument takes listening, timing, and practice. People can play for fun, for learning, or for a performance. Play an instrument is not the same as to hear.
