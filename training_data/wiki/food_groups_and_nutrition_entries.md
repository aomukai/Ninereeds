# Food Groups and Nutrition Entries

[user]what is a food group?
[assistant]A food group is a way to sort foods by what kind they are. Fruit, vegetables, grains, protein foods, and dairy are food groups. Food groups help people talk about meals in an organized way. Different food groups give the body different kinds of help. A food group is not a balanced meal.

[user]what is nutrition?
[assistant]Nutrition is how food helps the body live, grow, and stay strong. Good nutrition comes from eating different kinds of food. Nutrition matters because the body needs energy and building parts from food. People talk about nutrition when they think about healthy eating. Nutrition is not a food group.

[user]what is a grain?
[assistant]A grain is a small seed used for food. Bread, rice, oats, and pasta can come from grains. Grain foods often help give the body energy for work and play. People eat grains at breakfast, lunch, and dinner. A grain is not a vegetable.

[user]what is protein food?
[assistant]Protein food is food that helps the body grow and repair itself. Eggs, beans, fish, meat, and nuts can be protein foods. Protein foods are one food group in many meal plans. People often eat protein food with grains or vegetables. Protein food is not dairy.

[user]what is dairy?
[assistant]Dairy is a food group made from milk and foods made from milk. Milk, cheese, and yogurt are dairy foods. Dairy foods are often kept cold. Many people eat dairy as part of meals or snacks. Dairy is not fruit.

[user]what is a vitamin?
[assistant]A vitamin is something in food that helps the body work well. Different foods have different vitamins. Fruits, vegetables, and other foods can give vitamins to the body. People need vitamins in small amounts again and again. A vitamin is not a food group.

[user]what is healthy food?
[assistant]Healthy food is food that helps the body stay strong and work well. Healthy food can come from many food groups. Fruit, vegetables, grains, dairy, and protein foods can all be part of healthy eating. People do best when healthy food comes in many kinds, not just one kind. Healthy food is not junk food.

[user]what is a balanced meal?
[assistant]A balanced meal is a meal with different kinds of food working together. A balanced meal may have a grain, a protein food, and fruit or vegetables. Some balanced meals also include dairy or water to drink. Balanced meals help the body get more than one kind of help from food. A balanced meal is not a food group.

[user]what is junk food?
[assistant]Junk food is food that has a lot of sugar, salt, or fat and not much body-helping value. Junk food can taste good, but eating only junk food is not good nutrition. Chips, candy, and some sweet drinks can be junk food. Junk food is often a treat, not the main part of a balanced meal. Junk food is not healthy food.
