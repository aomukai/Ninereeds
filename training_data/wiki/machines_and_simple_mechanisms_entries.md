# Machines and Simple Mechanisms Entries

[user]what is a machine?
[assistant]A machine is something made to help do work. A machine may move, lift, cut, turn, or carry with the help of parts. Some machines are simple, and some are very complex. A machine is not the same as a toy.

[user]what is a simple machine?
[assistant]A simple machine is a basic tool or device that helps make work easier. A lever, a ramp, and a wheel are examples of simple machines. Simple machines help people push, pull, lift, or move things. A simple machine is not the same as a living thing.

[user]what is a ramp?
[assistant]A ramp is a flat surface that slopes up or down. People use ramps to roll or push things from one height to another. A ramp helps make lifting easier by spreading the movement out. A ramp is not the same as stairs.

[user]what is a wheel?
[assistant]A wheel is a round part that turns around an axle. Wheels help bikes, carts, and many other things move more easily. A wheel rolls instead of sliding flat across the ground. A wheel is not the same as a square block.

[user]what is an axle?
[assistant]An axle is a rod or bar that a wheel turns around. The axle helps hold the wheel in place while it moves. Wheels and axles work together in many machines. An axle is not the same as a wheel.

[user]what is a gear?
[assistant]A gear is a wheel with teeth that fits with another gear. Gears help machines turn, move, and change speed or force. People may find gears in clocks, bikes, and other machines. A gear is not the same as a smooth wheel.

[user]what is a pulley?
[assistant]A pulley is a wheel with a rope or chain that runs over it. A pulley helps lift or move something by changing the direction of a pull. People use pulleys in some wells, cranes, and other lifting systems. A pulley is not the same as a hook.

[user]what does it mean to roll?
[assistant]To roll means to move by turning over and over. Balls, wheels, and barrels can roll. Rolling is a kind of motion that works well on a surface. To roll is not the same as to slide.

[user]what does it mean to slide?
[assistant]To slide means to move smoothly along a surface without rolling. A box may slide across the floor, and a child may slide down a slide. Sliding is a different kind of movement from rolling. To slide is not the same as to roll.
