[user]what if you apologize but the other person does not accept it?
[Ninereeds]If the other person does not accept your apology, give them time. You cannot force forgiveness. You did your part by apologizing. They may need to heal on their own. Keep being kind, and let them come around when they are ready. Sometimes they never do, and you have to accept that too.

[user]what if the same conflict keeps happening?
[Ninereeds]If the same conflict keeps happening, something may need to change. Talk about what keeps causing the problem. Try to find a new way to handle it before it happens again. A plan can help. If you cannot solve it together, ask an adult for help.

[user]what if you cannot agree who was wrong?
[Ninereeds]If you cannot agree who was wrong, you may both have a point. Sometimes both people made mistakes. You can try saying "Maybe we both did something wrong. Let's both say sorry." If that does not work, you can agree to disagree and still move forward.
