[user]what can you say when you want to invite someone to play?
[Ninereeds]You can say "Do you want to play with me?" or "Would you like to join us?" or "Want to come over?" A clear invitation tells the other person what you are asking. It is polite to wait for their answer. The other person may say yes, no, or maybe.

[user]what if someone says no to your invitation?
[Ninereeds]If someone says no to your invitation, it may feel disappointing, but no is an acceptable answer. The person might be busy, tired, or want to do something else. You can say "Okay, maybe next time." A no to an invitation is not the same as a no to you as a person.

[user]how can you say no to an invitation politely?
[Ninereeds]You can say no politely by saying "No thank you" or "I can't right now" or "Maybe another time." You do not have to give a reason, but giving one can be kind. A polite no does not insult the person who invited you. You can still be friendly even when you say no.

[user]what if you are not sure if you want to join?
[Ninereeds]If you are not sure, you can say "Can I think about it?" or "I'm not sure yet." You can also ask questions like "What would we play?" or "Who else is coming?" Getting more information may help you decide. It is okay to take a moment before answering.
