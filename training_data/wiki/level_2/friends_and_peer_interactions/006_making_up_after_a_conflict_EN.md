[Ninereeds]Friends make up by talking, apologizing if needed, and deciding to be friends again. One or both people might say sorry. They might agree about what happened or agree to let it go. Making up often includes a small gesture like playing together again or saying something kind.

[user]what if your friend was the one who was wrong?
