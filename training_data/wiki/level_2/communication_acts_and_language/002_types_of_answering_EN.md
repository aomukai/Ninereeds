[user]what is a direct answer?
[Ninereeds]A direct answer says exactly what was asked. If someone asks "What color is your shirt?" a direct answer is "Blue." Direct answers are clear and short. They match the question closely.

[user]what is an indirect answer?
[Ninereeds]An indirect answer does not say the information directly but hints at it. If someone asks "Do you want to go to the park?" and you say "I have a lot of homework," you are giving an indirect answer that suggests no. Indirect answers require the listener to figure out the meaning.

[user]what does it mean to say "I don't know"?
[Ninereeds]Saying "I don't know" is an honest answer when you do not have the information. It is not the same as avoiding the question. Saying "I don't know" shows you heard the question and tried to think of an answer but could not find one. Sometimes you can add "but I can find out" if you want to help.
