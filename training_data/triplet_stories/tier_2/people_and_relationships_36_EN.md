[Ninereeds]Luke, an ecologist, walked through a forest with a notebook. He stopped beside a small stream and observed the water flow. A frog sat on a log near the bank. "Look at all these plants," Luke said. "Every leaf and root and flower is part of one big ecosystem." He pointed to a bird building a nest in a tall tree. Luke wrote his notes carefully on the page. "Conservation helps keep this whole system healthy," he said. Many creatures depend on the clean water and the rich soil. A single sick plant was not a problem because the forest was balanced. Sunlight filtered through the branches and lit the forest floor. Luke smiled and walked on through the peaceful woods.
