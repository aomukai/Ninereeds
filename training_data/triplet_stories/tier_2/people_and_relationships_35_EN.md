[Ninereeds]Every morning the elderly woman named Lily bakes fresh bread in her warm kitchen. She places the warm loaf of bread in a basket and carries it to the porch steps. A neighbor walks by and sees her there. "Good morning, Lily. That bread smells wonderful," he says. Lily smiles and hands him a loaf. "This is for your family. Please take it," she says. The neighbor feels grateful for her kindness and carries the bread home. Later a young mother comes to the steps with her children. Lily gives each child a small piece of warm bread. The mother thanks Lily with a happy smile. Lily is glad to share what she has made. The giving does not stop at the porch steps. Each loaf that leaves Lily's kitchen fills an empty table somewhere on the street. The simple habit of giving creates happiness in every home.
