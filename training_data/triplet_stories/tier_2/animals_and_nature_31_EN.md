[Ninereeds]Sophie liked the ivy on the old stone wall in her backyard. Each morning she walked out to see the green leaves. Tiny new buds appeared along the stems. "Look how small they are," she said. The buds unfolded in the warm spring sun. Rain helped the steady growth of the vines across the wall. Sophie touched a pale green leaf. "You are growing so well," she whispered. The ivy climbed up and covered more of the brick. Morning light made the leaves look bright and pretty. Sophie watched the growth from week to week. Soon the wall was a thick blanket of green. The girl smiled at her quiet green friend.
