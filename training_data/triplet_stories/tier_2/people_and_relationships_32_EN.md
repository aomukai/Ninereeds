[Ninereeds]Ella is an architect who designs buildings for the community. She worked for many months on her design for a new library. The library has large windows that let in the morning sun. It has open rooms where families can gather and read. One morning, Ella walked through the front door with a group of children. A young girl looked up at the tall walls and asked, "Did you build all of this?" Ella smiled and said, "I drew the plan, and many people worked together to make it real." She showed the children the quiet reading room with its soft light. She pointed to the shelves filled with books and happy readers. The creation stood bright and welcoming for everyone. Children sat at tables with their books and smiled. Ella felt proud of her work. She knew the library would serve the community for many years.
