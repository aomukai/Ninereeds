[Ninereeds]Luke looked at the ripe vegetables growing in the garden and saw an opportunity. He asked the gardener, "May I sell these at the market?" The gardener nodded and said, "You have my permission." Luke picked tomatoes and carrots and packed them into a basket. He walked to the town market and set up a small table. A neighbor stopped and asked, "How much for the tomatoes?" Luke named a fair price, and the neighbor placed coins in his hand. Another customer bought carrots with a warm smile. Each sale was a small acceptance that made the opportunity real. Luke counted what he had earned and felt a quiet joy. Permission had opened the door, and acceptance had walked through it.
