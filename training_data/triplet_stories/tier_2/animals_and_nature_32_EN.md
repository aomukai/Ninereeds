[Ninereeds]A narrow trail winds through the quiet countryside. Toby walks along the trail with a bucket in his hand. The path is dusty and the sun is warm on his head. He walks past a green field with an old wooden fence. A cow stands near the fence and looks at him. "Are you thirsty too?" Toby asks the cow. The cow does not answer, so Toby keeps walking. He reaches a stone trough at the edge of the next field. The trough has only a little water at the bottom. Toby fills his bucket from a nearby tap and pours the fresh water into the trough. The cow follows him and drinks the cool water slowly. Toby smiles and rests by the trough for a minute. The countryside is calm and still around him.
