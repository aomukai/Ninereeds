[Ninereeds]Noah gathers with other children in the garden to plant vegetables. Noah digs a long row of holes with a small shovel. A friend places seeds into each hole. Another child covers the seeds with soil. Noah stands up and asks, "Can everyone help water the plants?" His friends nod and carry water from the spigot. They work side by side in the warm afternoon sun. Each child gives effort to the task. The garden begins to grow after a few weeks. Tiny green shoots push through the soil. The children smile at what they built together. Unity makes the garden grow strong. Noah feels proud of his team.
