[Ninereeds]Lily looks at a jar of marbles on the table. The jar holds five red marbles and three blue marbles. Lily asks her father, "What is the chance I pick a red marble?" Her father explains that probability describes how likely each pull is to produce a red marble. The chance of picking a red marble is five out of eight. A blue marble is less likely to appear. Lily reaches into the jar. She pulls out a blue marble and smiles. "I got the less likely one!" she says. Her father nods and says the outcome of one pull is not certain until she looks at her hand.
