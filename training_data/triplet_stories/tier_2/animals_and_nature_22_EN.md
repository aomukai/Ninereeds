[Ninereeds]Lily stood on the porch and watched a flock of birds cross the evening scenery. Her father came out and stood beside her. "Look at them fly," Lily said. Her father gave a smile. "They are looking for a place to settle for the night." One bird made a swift swoop toward the field below. The wings of the birds caught the last light of the sun. All the birds moved together like one wide wave in the air. The shape of the flock was not a fixed pattern. It shifted and changed as the birds flew. Lily held her coat and watched until the flock was gone. The dusk held the whole scenery in a quiet moment of motion.
