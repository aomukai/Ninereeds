[Ninereeds]Toby arrived at the building site early. A truck had delivered a load of fresh concrete. Toby grabbed his shovel and walked toward the wet pile. "We need to move fast before it sets," he said. His partner nodded and picked up a rake. Together they spread the concrete into the wooden forms on the ground. Toby used a long trowel to smooth the surface. Every component of the foundation had to be level. The production of a strong slab takes careful timing. Toby checked the edge and nodded. "Good work for one morning," his partner said. The concrete would cure over the next few days. It was a key component of the new garden path.
