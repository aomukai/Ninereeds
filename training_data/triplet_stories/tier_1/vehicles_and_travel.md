## 161 car + road + drive
The sun is bright today. A blue car sits in the driveway. Dad turns the key and starts to drive. The tires hum on the long, gray road. We pass green trees as we move along. Everything looks very small through the window. The car goes faster and faster. We are moving down the road now.

## 162 bus + stop + ride
A big yellow bus comes around the corner. It pulls up slowly next to the bus stop. I wait by the tall sign with my bag. The doors creak open wide. We hop inside for a bumpy ride. Everyone finds a seat near the window. The bus moves away from the curb. My friends are sitting right behind me.

## 163 bike + wheel + ride
I grab my shiny red bike from the garage. One round wheel spins in the wind. I push hard on the pedals to ride. My feet move up and down quickly. The front wheel turns left and right. A little bell rings as I go. The ride feels very smooth on the path. I can see the flowers passing by.

## 164 train + track + fast
The big engine whistles loudly. It rolls along the heavy metal track. Look at the long train moving past us! It goes so fast that it makes a loud sound. The wheels click-clack on the rails. We watch the cars zoom by the station. Everything is a blur of color. The train is rushing down the line.

## 165 plane + sky + fly
Look up at the bright blue sky! A tiny white plane appears near a cloud. It starts to fly higher and higher. The pilot looks out at the world below. We see the wings spread wide. Everything on the ground looks like a toy. The plane glides through the air. It is soaring way up high.

## 166 boat + water + float
The blue water ripples in the sun. A small wooden boat sits on the lake. It stays steady and starts to float. I watch it bob up and down. The waves are very gentle today. We use a long oar to move across. The boat drifts near the lily pads. Water splashes against the side.

## 167 truck + load + carry
The engine of the big truck rumbles. It has a massive load in the back. Heavy boxes sit high on the flatbed. The driver must carry them to the store. The wheels turn slowly under the weight. Dust kicks up from the dirt. The truck moves down the street. We watch the heavy cargo go by.

## 168 sled + snow + slide
The ground is covered in white snow. I sit on my bright red sled. I push off the top of the hill. Watch me slide down so fast! The cold wind hits my face. Snow flies up behind me. It feels like flying on the ice. I am zooming toward the bottom.

## 169 wagon + pull + carry
The wooden wagon is empty right now. I grab the rope to pull it along. We need to carry all these toys. My dog runs beside us in the grass. The wheels squeak as we move. It is heavy when it is full. I tug the wagon forward again. We are bringing everything inside.

## 170 ship + sea + sail
The ocean is very big and blue. A giant ship moves through the waves. It starts to sail toward the horizon. The wind blows against the tall masts. White foam splashes around the hull. Sailors work on the wide deck. We watch it disappear into the mist. The ship is crossing the deep sea.

## 171 wheel + roll + round
The toy car has a bright orange wheel. It is shaped in a perfect round way. I push it and watch it roll. It spins faster across the floor. The wheel stays straight as it moves. Round things are fun to play with. It bumps into a wooden block. The motion never seems to stop.

## 172 road + cross + car
The black road is very wide today. Many cars drive past the park. I stand at the edge of the street. We wait for the lights to change. Now we can safely cross the way. A red car stops at the line. We walk quickly to the other side. The traffic moves behind us.

## 173 bridge + water + cross
The river flows fast underneath us. A tall bridge reaches from one side to the other. I walk across the metal path. Looking down, the water looks very deep. The structure feels strong under my feet. We can cross the river so easily here. Birds fly under the big beams. We reach the other side.

## 174 helmet + bike + safe
I put on my bright blue helmet. It fits snugly around my head. Now I am ready to ride my bike. The straps keep me feeling very safe. I pedal down the bumpy path. My head feels protected by the hard shell. I zoom past the big oak tree. Riding is much better with it on.

## 175 seatbelt + car + safe
We sit down in the back seat. Mom pulls the nylon seatbelt across me. It clicks loudly into the metal buckle. This keeps us very safe in the car. The car starts to move down the lane. I hold my teddy bear tight. We are ready for our trip. The engine hums softly.

## 176 walk + foot + slow
The garden path is very narrow. I take a step with my left foot. Then I move my right foot forward. We walk very slow through the flowers. Each tiny step feels quite careful. A snail crawls on a leaf nearby. My feet crunch on the dry dirt. The stroll is very peaceful.

## 177 run + fast + race
The whistle blows loudly at the start. I begin to run as hard as I can. My legs move very fast. This is a big race today! I try to pass the other kids. My breath comes in quick puffs. I am moving toward the finish line. The crowd is cheering loudly.

## 178 trip + travel + far
We pack our bags for a big trip. It is time to travel to the mountains. We drive through many different towns. The view out the window changes often. We are going so far from home. Maps and snacks are in the car. I wonder what we will see. The road stretches on forever.

## 179 map + find + way
The paper map is spread on the table. It has many colorful lines on it. We use it to find the right way. I point to a little blue star. That is where our house is! The symbols show us where to turn. We follow the path carefully. Now we know exactly where to go.

## 180 stop + sign + wait
The car approaches the corner slowly. A red stop sign stands by the road. The driver presses the brake pedal. We must wait for a moment. Other cars are passing by now. I look out at the quiet street. Everything stays still for a second. Then, we can move again.
