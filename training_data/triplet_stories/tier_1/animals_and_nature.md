## 1 bird + nest + tree
High up in a big green tree, a little bird lives. She flies around to find small twigs. One by one, the bird brings them back. Soon, she builds a cozy round nest. The nest stays safe among the leaves. A tiny egg might sit inside very soon. The wind blows softly through the branches. The bird settles down to rest.

## 2 frog + pond + leaf
A green frog jumps near the edge of the water. He looks at the big, flat leaf floating by. With a quick hop, the frog lands right on top! The leaf wobbles on the pond. He sits very still and watches the water. Tiny ripples move across the surface. A small bug flies past his eyes. The frog waits for it to land.

## 3 bunny + carrot + garden
A fluffy bunny hops through the garden. The sun feels warm on his soft ears. Suddenly, he smells something yummy in the dirt. He digs with his little paws to find a bright orange carrot. The bunny grabs the carrot and starts to munch. Crunch, crunch, crunch! Leaves fly everywhere as he eats. He is a very happy bunny.

## 4 fish + water + worm
Deep in the blue water, a small fish swims. He wiggles his fins to move along. Down at the bottom, he sees a wiggly worm. The worm moves through the sand and bubbles. Quickly, the fish swims toward it! He opens his mouth wide and gobbles the worm up. Bubbles float up to the top. The fish keeps swimming happily.

## 5 bee + flower + flower
A fuzzy bee flies through the bright air. She sees a beautiful red flower in the grass. She lands softly on the petals to find food. As she drinks, she makes sweet honey inside her hive. The flower sways in the breeze. More yellow pollen sticks to her tiny legs. She buzzes loudly and flies away to find another one.

## 6 dog + ball + grass
A happy dog runs fast across the green grass. He sees a bright red ball rolling away! His ears flop as he chases it down. The ball bounces over a small bump. The dog catches up and grabs it in his mouth. He wags his tail very quickly. He drops the ball and waits to play again.

## 7 cat + mouse + home
Inside the quiet home, a cat walks on soft paws. She stays very low to the ground. Suddenly, she sees a tiny mouse peeking out! The mouse scurries behind a big chair. The cat watches closely with her bright eyes. She creeps closer and closer to the corner. The mouse stops for a second to look around.

## 8 duck + pond + bread
A yellow duck swims in the middle of the pond. He sees something floating near the shore. It is a tasty piece of bread! The duck paddles his feet very fast to get there. He reaches the bread and takes a big bite. The water splashes around his tummy. He keeps eating happily in the sun.

## 9 butterfly + flower + sun
A colorful butterfly flutters through the air. The bright sun shines down on everything. She finds a pretty flower to rest on. Her wings move slowly as she drinks nectar. It is a very warm and sleepy afternoon. The butterfly stays still on the petal. A gentle breeze moves her wings just a little bit.

## 10 spider + web + fly
A tiny spider spins a long, thin web. She works hard to make the lines strong. The web hangs between two branches. Suddenly, a fast fly lands right in the middle! The web shakes as the fly wiggles around. The spider crawls out very quickly. She catches the fly in her sticky trap.

## 11 ant + hill + food
A tiny ant finds a big piece of food. It is much bigger than him! He carries it carefully across the dirt. He is walking toward his giant ant hill. Many other ants are busy working nearby. He reaches the entrance and drops his prize. The other ants come to help him move it inside.

## 12 squirrel + tree + nut
A bushy squirrel climbs up a tall tree. He carries something small in his mouth. It is a crunchy brown nut! He finds a perfect spot on a high branch. The squirrel hides the nut deep in a hole. He pats the bark with his paws. Now, he has a secret snack for later.

## 13 turtle + shell + rock
A slow turtle crawls over the warm ground. He finds a big, flat rock to rest on. He pulls his head inside his hard shell. The shell keeps him very safe and cozy. He feels the warmth of the sun on his back. Everything is quiet around the rock. The turtle stays tucked away for a nap.

## 14 owl + night + tree
The sky turns dark as night comes. An owl wakes up in a hollow tree. He opens his big, round eyes and looks down. The forest is very quiet and still. The owl spreads his wings to fly. He glides silently from the tree branch. He searches for something to eat in the dark.

## 15 horse + field + hay
A large horse stands in a wide, open field. He hears the sound of footsteps nearby. Someone brings him a pile of dry, yellow hay. The horse leans down to take a big bite. He chews the hay slowly and happily. His long tail swishes back and forth. The wind blows through his mane.

## 16 cow + farm + milk
A big cow walks around the busy farm. She stops near a wooden fence to eat grass. Later, she produces fresh, white milk. The milk goes into a clean bucket nearby. The sun is bright over the barn. The cow moos loudly to say hello. She continues to munch on the green clover.

## 17 sheep + wool + meadow
A fluffy sheep wanders through a grassy meadow. She eats the sweet clover all day long. Her body grows thick, soft wool. It covers her like a warm, white blanket. The sheep walks slowly through the tall grass. A little lamb follows close behind her. The meadow is very peaceful today.

## 18 chicken + egg + barn
A red chicken walks inside the big barn. She finds a quiet corner filled with straw. Suddenly, she lays a smooth white egg! The egg rests safely on the floor. The chicken pecks at some seeds nearby. She makes a happy clucking sound. The barn is warm and smells like hay.

## 19 worm + soil + rain
Deep down in the dark soil, a worm hides. Suddenly, heavy rain starts to fall from the sky. The ground gets very wet and soft. The worm feels the damp earth all around him. He crawls up out of the soil to breathe. The rain makes little splashes on the dirt. He moves slowly through the mud.

## 20 snail + shell + leaf
A little snail crawls across a bright green leaf. He moves very, very slowly. A heavy shell sits on his back. The leaf hangs from a small plant in the garden. As the snail moves, he leaves a shiny, wet trail behind him. He reaches the edge of the leaf and stops. The sun makes his trail sparkle.
