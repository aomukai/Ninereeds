# Play and Games — Tier 3 Stories

Tier 3 stories for play and games introduce causal reasoning, contrasts, and multi-paragraph structures while maintaining child-readable prose.

[Ninereeds]Luke finds a bright red ball on the grass near the fence. He picks it up because he wants to throw it high. The ball feels smooth and round in his hands, but it is a little bit wet from the morning dew.

Luke throws the ball into the air and watches it spin. "Catch it?" he asks. The ball goes up very high, so Luke has to run backward to catch it.

He stretches out his arms and waits. The ball lands in his hands instead of falling to the ground. Luke smiles because he made a good catch. He throws the ball again to practice some more.
