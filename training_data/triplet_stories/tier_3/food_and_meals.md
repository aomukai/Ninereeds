# Food and Meals — Tier 3 Stories

Tier 3 stories for food and meals introduce causal reasoning, contrasts, and multi-paragraph structures while maintaining child-readable prose.

"This apple is perfect," Beth says. She polishes the apple on her shirt and takes a big bite. The apple is sweet, but it is also a little tart near the skin.

Beth eats the whole apple down to the core. She throws the core into the garden, so the seeds might grow into new trees. Her grandmother says apple trees take a long time to grow, but Beth likes to imagine a whole orchard someday.
