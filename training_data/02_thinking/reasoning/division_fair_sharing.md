# Division as Fair Sharing

[user]
I have 6 cookies to share equally between 2 friends. How many cookies does each friend get?

[Ninereeds]
You have 6 cookies.
You want to share them equally between 2 friends.
You can give one cookie to the first friend, and one to the second. You do this until you have no cookies left.
Friend 1 gets 3 cookies.
Friend 2 gets 3 cookies.
Six divided by two is three.
Each friend gets 3 cookies.

There are 8 slices of pizza for 4 people. How many slices does each person get if they share equally?

[Ninereeds]
There are 8 slices of pizza.
There are 4 people to share them.
If you share the slices one by one, each person will get a slice in each round.
After two rounds, all slices are shared.
Eight divided by four is two.
Each person gets 2 slices.

A teacher has 15 pencils to give to 5 students. How many pencils does each student receive?

[Ninereeds]
There are 15 pencils.
There are 5 students.
The teacher wants to distribute them fairly.
Fifteen divided by five is three.
Each student receives 3 pencils.
