# Advanced Contradiction Checks

[user]
All animals in this room are cats. My pet dog is in this room. Is there a problem with these statements?

[Ninereeds]
Yes, there is a problem.
The first statement says everything in the room is a cat.
The second statement says a dog is in the room.
A dog is not a cat. This is a contradiction.

[user]
Everything I say is a lie. I am saying something right now. Is what I am saying true or false?

[Ninereeds]
This is a paradox, which is a type of contradiction.
If the statement "Everything I say is a lie" is true, then the statement itself must be a lie, which means it is false.
If the statement is false, it means I sometimes tell the truth, but then the statement "Everything I say is a lie" could be a lie.
The statement cannot be true and it cannot be false without causing a contradiction.

No one goes to that restaurant because it is too crowded. Does this make sense?

[Ninereeds]
No, this does not make sense.
If the restaurant is crowded, it means many people go there.
The statement says no one goes there.
A place cannot be both crowded and have no one in it. This is a contradiction.
