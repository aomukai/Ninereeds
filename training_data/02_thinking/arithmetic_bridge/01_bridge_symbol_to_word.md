[user]what does 1 mean?

[user]what does + mean?
[Ninereeds]+ means plus. It is the sign for addition.
When you see +, two numbers are being joined together. The total gets bigger.
+ is not the same as -.

[user]what does - mean?
[Ninereeds]- means minus. It is the sign for subtraction.
When you see -, a number is being taken away. The amount gets smaller.
- is not the same as +.

[user]what does = mean?
[Ninereeds]= means equals. It means both sides are the same amount.
The left side and the right side match. = does not mean more or less.

[user]what does 1 + 1 = 2 mean?
[Ninereeds]It means one plus one equals two.
One group of one is joined with another group of one. The total is two.
Both sides of the = sign are the same amount.

[user]what does 2 + 3 = 5 mean?
[Ninereeds]It means two plus three equals five.
Two and three are joined together. The total is five.
Both sides of the = sign are the same amount.

[user]what does 5 - 2 = 3 mean?
[Ninereeds]It means five minus two equals three.
Two is taken away from five. Three is what remains.
Both sides of the = sign are the same amount.

[user]what does 4 - 4 = 0 mean?
[Ninereeds]It means four minus four equals zero.
When you take all of a number away, nothing is left. The result is zero.
4 - 4 = 0 is always true.

[user]what does 3 + 0 = 3 mean?
[Ninereeds]It means three plus zero equals three.
Adding zero does not change the number. Three stays three.
Zero does not add anything to the group.

[user]what does 6 - 0 = 6 mean?
[Ninereeds]It means six minus zero equals six.
Taking away zero does not change the number. Six stays six.
Zero does not remove anything from the group.

[user]what does 2 + 3 = 3 + 2 mean?
[Ninereeds]It means two plus three is the same as three plus two.
Both equal five. The order of adding does not change the total.
Joining two groups gives the same result no matter which group comes first.
