Clara set three things on the nature table.

A feather. A stone. A bone.

Emma stepped closer. She did not touch. She looked.

The feather was grey and soft at the edge. It had a quill that was clear and hollow.

The stone was grey too, but rough. It sat on the cloth and did not move.

The bone was small and white. It had a curve to it. One end was smooth. The other end had a knob.

Clara said nothing. She waited.

Emma pointed at the stone. "This was never alive."

Mei looked at the bone. She touched it with one finger. "This was."

Clara brought two trays from the cupboard. She put a card on each tray.

One card said *Was Alive*.

One card said *Was Never Alive*.

Emma picked up the feather. She turned it in her fingers. The quill was light. The barbs were soft. She put it on the first tray.

Mei picked up the bone. It was heavier than it looked. She laid it beside the feather.

Emma picked up the stone. It was cold. It did not change in her hand. She put it on the other tray.

Clara looked at the trays. She nodded once.

The feather and the bone sat together. The stone sat alone.
