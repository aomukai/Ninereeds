Dou sat on the garden wall. Her tail was straight up. One white ear pointed forward.

Bello saw her from the gate. He stopped. His ears went up.

He took a step closer. Then another. Then he whined.

Dou did not move.

Bello took a step back. He looked at Taro. He whined again.

“She is not interested,” Mei said.

Bello took a step forward again. Then back. His front paws shifted. He could not decide.

Under the apple tree, Biscuit lay with his head on his paws. He did not turn his head. He did not even lift an ear.

Taro watched Bello. The small dog wanted something. He did not know what to do with it.

Bello crouched. He wagged his tail once. Then he stood up and whined again.

Dou blinked. That was all.

Mei sat down on the grass. “He will try all afternoon,” she said.

Taro saw that she was right. Bello could not let it go. The cat was there. He had to do something about it.

He stepped forward again. Dou looked past him at the apple tree.

Biscuit yawned.
