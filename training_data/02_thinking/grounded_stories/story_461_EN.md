Dou sat on the old bench at the pond. She was in a patch of afternoon sun. Her grey fur was warm. Her tail was still. She did not move.

Bello came down the meadow path. He trotted to the flat stone by the water. He stopped. He saw Dou on the bench. His ears went up. His tail did not wag.

He did not go closer.

Bello stepped off the stone. He turned. He took a wide path around the edge of the pond. His paws were quiet on the grass. He did not look at the bench. He walked to the far side of the pond and stood there.

Dou stayed on the bench. She did not turn her head. She did not watch him pass. She sat in the sun and did not move.

The pond was still. A fly went over the water. Dou blinked once.

Bello stayed on the far side. He did not come back to the flat stone. The bench was warm. The sun was low now. Nothing else happened.
