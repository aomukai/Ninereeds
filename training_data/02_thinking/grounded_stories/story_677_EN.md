The path was all mud. Puddles sat in every hole and dip.

Biscuit did not stop. He went straight through the first puddle. Water flew up. Then the next. Then the next. His legs were brown. His belly was brown. His tail wagged, splashing more.

Taro did not go straight. He jumped over some puddles. He landed in others on purpose. The mud went up his boots. He laughed and stepped in a deep one. Water came over the top.

Emma walked around the deep puddles. She found a dry bit of path, then another. She stepped on a flat stone. She stepped on a clump of grass. Her boots stayed clean.

Bello came last. He picked his way along the edge. He put one paw down, then the other, on the driest ground he could find. A puddle stretched across the whole path. Bello stopped. He tried the grass. Too wet. He tried the mud. Too deep. He sat down and waited.

Taro looked back. "Come on."

Bello did not move.

Emma went back. She picked him up. His paws were still clean. She carried him past the deep puddle and set him down on dry path.

Bello shook his whole body. Then he followed the edge again.
