Stefan walked the main street of the village. His hands were clasped behind his back. The sun made his badge shine.

Biscuit pulled on the lead. He pulled toward Stefan with his nose low and his tail high.

Stefan stopped. He bent down and put his hand on Biscuit's head. He patted him once, firmly, the way Biscuit liked.

Bello circled back. He did not go straight to Stefan like Biscuit had. He came from the side and sniffed Stefan's boot. Then he sniffed the bottom of his trouser leg.

"I know you," Stefan said. He said Biscuit's name. Then he said Bello's name.

The dogs looked at him. Biscuit's tail wagged. Bello sat down.

Stefan straightened up. He put his hands behind his back again. He walked on down the street.

The dogs stayed still. They watched him go. His boots made a steady sound on the stones. The sun was on his back, and the badge was a small bright spot that moved away.

Emma and Taro waited on the bench. Biscuit turned at last and came back to them. Bello took one more look down the street, then followed.
