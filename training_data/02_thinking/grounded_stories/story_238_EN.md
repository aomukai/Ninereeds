The stream ran past the mill. It was clear and fast.

Mei stood on the bank. Emma stood beside her.

An orange leaf fell. It landed on the water.

"Look," said Mei.

The leaf turned in the current. It drifted toward the big wheel.

Another leaf fell. Yellow. Then another.

They floated side by side. The water carried them.

Biscuit stood at the edge. He watched the leaves go by.

The wheel turned slowly. Water dripped from the paddles.

A leaf caught on one of the wooden slats. It stuck there.

Another leaf caught above it. Orange and yellow against the dark wood.

The wheel kept turning. The leaves turned with it.

Biscuit leaned forward. He snapped at a leaf as it passed.

He missed.

The leaf kept going. It floated away downstream.

Mei pointed. "The wheel has spots now."

Emma looked. The paddles were dotted with colour. Orange, yellow, a few brown ones.

Biscuit tried again. He snapped at a yellow leaf.

He caught it.

He shook his head. The leaf fell into the stream and floated away.

The wheel turned. More leaves fell. The water carried them.
