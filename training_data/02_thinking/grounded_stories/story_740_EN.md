Emma held the egg in her hand.

It was cold and smooth. She held it over the bowl.

Her hand shook.

The egg hit the rim of the bowl. It slipped. It fell past her fingers and hit the floor. The shell cracked open. Yellow ran across the stone. White spread around it.

Emma's face went red. Her jaw was tight. She did not say anything.

Gran looked up from the table. She did not say anything either. She reached into the basket and took out another egg. She held it out.

Emma looked at the egg on the floor. She looked at Gran's hand.

She took the egg.

She held it tight. She stood still. She breathed in. She felt the shell against her palm. She breathed out slow.

She held the egg over the bowl. Her hand was still. She tapped it once on the rim. The shell cracked clean. She pulled the two halves apart. The yolk dropped into the bowl. The white followed.

Gran nodded. She picked up a cloth and knelt to wipe the floor.

Emma looked at the egg in the bowl. She looked at her hand. It was not shaking now.
