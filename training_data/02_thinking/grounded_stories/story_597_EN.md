The sun was high and very hot. The pond lay still and flat, like a sheet of glass that had forgotten to move.

Emma sat on the flat stone. It was warm under her legs. She leaned forward and looked down. Six fish hung near the bottom, dark and slow. They did not swim. They just stayed there, as if the heat had stopped them.

Bello stood in the shallows. His front paws were wet. He looked at the water and then at the children, and then at the water again. He took one step deeper. The water came up to his knees.

Biscuit did not go near the water. He walked to the willow and lay down in the shade, flat on his side, tongue out and breathing fast.

Taro knelt at the edge. He put his hand into the water. He kept it there for a moment.

"It's cooler," he said.

Emma looked up. A frog sat on a lily pad, very still. Its throat moved once.

Bello dipped his nose into the water and sneezed.

The willow leaves hung straight down. Nothing moved. The sun was very high and the pond was very still.
