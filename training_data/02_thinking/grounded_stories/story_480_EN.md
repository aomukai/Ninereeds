Mei sat on the stone bench by the herb trough. Her hands were still. She was watching Dou.

Dou sat on the wall above the bench. One white ear turned. Her tail was upright. She blinked slowly.

Bello lay on the path near the bench. His head was low on his front paws. His eyes did not leave the cat.

Taro stopped at the garden gate. He waited. No barks. No chases.

Gran pulled weeds from the vegetable patch. She did not look up.

Taro walked past Biscuit under the apple tree. Biscuit was asleep. One ear twitched. That was all.

Taro stood beside the bench. "They are not fighting," he said.

Mei shook her head. "Dou does not fight. She sits."

Bello's tail moved once. A small sweep. He did not get up.

Dou turned her head. She looked at Bello. Then she looked at the sky.

Taro sat down on the path. Bello's nose was near his knee. The cat was on the wall. The other dog was asleep.

Gran pulled another weed. The garden was warm. The dogs and the cat were in the same space. Not friends. Not enemies. Just near.
