The corridor had a row of hooks. Each hook had a card above it with a name.

Taro pulled off his coat. He let it fall. It landed on the floor in a heap.

Emma hung her coat on her hook. Mei hung hers on the hook that said MEI. She smoothed the collar flat.

Clara came out of the classroom. She looked at the floor.

"Taro," she said. She pointed at the card above his hook.

Taro looked up. The card said TARO in careful letters.

He picked up his coat. He hung it on the peg. The coat hung straight. It matched the label.

Mei took off her scarf. She folded it once and laid it over the coat. Her boots went side by side underneath.

Emma's scarf hung from her peg. It did not reach the floor.

Clara nodded and went back into the classroom.

Taro looked at his coat. It was not folded. But it was on the hook. That was where it belonged.
