The board was long and pale. Vern put it on the bench and handed Emma a ruler.

She put the ruler at the edge. The numbers started there. She found the length and made a mark with the pencil. The line was small and straight.

Then she turned the ruler around. She measured from the other side of the board. The ruler stopped at a different place. Her second mark was not where the first mark had been.

She frowned. She looked at the two marks. They were not the same.

Vern did not say anything. He stood beside the bench with his hands loose.

Emma measured again from the first end. She watched the numbers. She held the pencil at the right place. Then she looked at the second mark she had made. It was wrong. She rubbed it out with her thumb and made a new mark beside the first one.

She measured from the second end once more. The ruler stopped at the new mark. The same place.

She looked up. Vern nodded once. Then he picked up the saw.
