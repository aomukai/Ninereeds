The rain had stopped. The meadow path was wet.

A puddle lay in the dip where the stones had sunk. It was wide and still. The sky sat in it, pale and grey.

Taro found a pebble. He held it out over the water and let go.

*Plink.*

A circle spread from where it had gone in. It grew wider and wider, then faded.

Mei picked up a pebble. She dropped it a little to the left.

*Plink.*

Her circle spread. It met Taro's circle where it was still fading. For a moment the two ripples crossed each other, one over the other, then both were gone.

"Did you see that?" Taro said.

Mei nodded. She dropped another pebble. Taro dropped another. They watched the circles spread and meet and spread again.

Taro counted. "One, two, three, four, five."

Bello stepped into the puddle. His front paws went in. The water splashed. All the circles broke at once. He stood there looking at his own feet.

The ripples stopped. The water was still again.

Taro held out another pebble. "Wait," he said.

They waited. The water went flat. The sky settled back into it.

He dropped the pebble.
