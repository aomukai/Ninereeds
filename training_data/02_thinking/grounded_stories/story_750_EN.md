Vern stood at his workbench. Sawdust covered the floor in a pale layer. He took the broom and swept it into a pile in the middle of the room.

A nail fell. It made a small sound — *tink* — and was gone.

Vern looked down at the sawdust. He could not see the nail anywhere. He put the broom against the wall and walked to the shelf. Emma watched him pick up a grey stone and bring it back.

He knelt beside the sawdust pile. He passed the stone just above it, very slowly.

Something moved under the sawdust. A small dark shape stirred, then jumped up and stuck to the bottom of the stone. The nail hung there, held tight.

Vern turned the stone over. The nail stayed. He took hold of it and pulled it sideways, not straight up. It came free in his fingers. He dropped it into the metal box with a *clink*.

Emma looked at the stone. "Can I try?"

Vern handed it to her. It was heavier than she expected. She held it over the sawdust pile and moved it slowly. Three more nails rose up from the pale dust and stuck underneath, one after another.
