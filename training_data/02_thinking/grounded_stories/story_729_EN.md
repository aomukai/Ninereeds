Emma stood still in the garden. She heard something.

A sound. Low. It came from the apple tree.

She turned her head. The sound was there. She took a step. It was louder.

Biscuit sniffed at the grass. Bello watched a leaf.

Emma walked toward the tree. The sound grew. It buzzed. It moved in the air.

She stopped under the branches. The tree was full of white blossom. Small petals fell on her hair.

She looked. She could not see what made the sound.

The buzzing came close. Then it went away. Then it came back.

She looked at a flower. A cluster of white petals. Something moved inside.

She leaned in. Her eyes found it.

A bee.

It crawled out of the flower. Its legs were heavy with yellow. It stopped on the edge of the petal.

The buzzing stopped.

The bee sat still. The air was quiet.

Then the bee lifted. The buzzing started again, loud and close, and the bee flew away over the garden wall.

Emma watched it go. Then she looked at the flower again. It was still open. There was nothing inside now.
