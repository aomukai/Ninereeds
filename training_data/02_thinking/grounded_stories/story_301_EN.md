Mei placed a potato on the brass scale. The arm tipped down on her side.

She reached into the basket and picked another. Smaller. She set it beside the first. The arm lifted a little but stayed low.

Emma watched the movement. Heavy on one side. Lighter on the other. The scale did not balance.

Mei looked at the potatoes. She took the first one off and put the second one on alone. The arm tipped the other way.

She put them both back in the basket and chose a third.

Emma leaned closer. The scale arm moved. It stopped in the middle.

Yun waited with her hand out.

Mei put the potato into Yun's palm. Yun placed it on the scale. The arm stayed level.

"Three hundred grams," Yun said.

Mei took the potato and put it into a paper bag. She looked at Emma. "You choose one."

Emma looked into the basket. The potatoes were all brown and dusty. She picked the one on top.

She put it on the scale. The arm dropped.

Yun waited.

Emma picked a second potato from the basket. Smaller. She set it beside the first. The arm lifted a little.

She looked at the scale. She looked at the potatoes. She took the first one off.

The arm swung up.

She put the first one back on.

The arm stayed level.

Yun held out her hand.
