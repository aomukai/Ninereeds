The sun was hot. The grass was dry under it. The oak stood at the edge of the field, and under it the ground was dark and cool.

Emma sat on a big root in the shade. The bark was rough against her legs. She put her hand on the ground. The earth was damp and cold.

Taro stood in the sun. He squinted. His arms were warm. The top of his head felt very hot.

He took one step. Into the shade.

The air changed. It was cooler on his face. His arms were not hot anymore. He stood still and felt the difference.

He stepped back into the sun. The heat came back at once. It was like standing near the oven door.

He stepped into the shade again. Cool. He stepped out. Hot. He did it again.

Biscuit lay down in the shade and put his nose on his paws. Bello walked to the edge of the shade and stopped. He put one paw into the sun and pulled it back.

Emma watched Taro step in and out. She did not say anything. The shade was good where she was.
