Stefan was walking his beat. His hands were behind his back. His badge caught the sun.

Biscuit and Bello trotted ahead of Emma and Taro. Biscuit’s nose was to the ground. Bello’s ears stood up like little flags.

Stefan stopped. He looked down.

“Biscuit,” he said. “Bello.”

He said their names without thinking. Like he had known them a long time.

Biscuit looked up. His tail went once — left, right.

Stefan bent. He put his hand on Biscuit’s head. One pat. Not hard. Just a pat.

Bello wagged his tail. He stepped closer. Stefan touched his ear.

Then Stefan straightened. He nodded at Emma. He nodded at Taro.

“Morning,” he said.

Then he kept walking. His hands went back behind his back. His badge caught the sun again.

The dogs watched him go. Biscuit sneezed. Bello shook his ears.

Then they trotted on, ahead of Emma and Taro, down the main street.
