Mei walked down the meadow path. Dou followed, a small grey shape just behind her heels.

At the pond, Dou stopped. She stood at the edge where the grass turned to mud. She sat down.

The water was still. The sky sat on top of it, pale and quiet. A reed bent near the bank. Dou watched it. Her ears turned forward. Her tail curled around her front paws.

Mei sat on the flat stone. She waited.

Dou did not put a paw in. She did not lean forward. She sat, and she watched. The water did not move. A small fish flicked near the surface and was gone.

Dou blinked. She did not chase it.

A dragonfly landed on the tip of a reed. Dou's whiskers twitched. She did not move.

Mei watched the cat watch the water. The willow dipped low on the far side. The reeds stood still.

Then Dou stood up. She turned in a slow circle. She sat back down in the same spot, facing the same water.

Mei smiled. She stayed on the flat stone. The pond stayed still. The cat stayed at the edge, watching something only she could see.
