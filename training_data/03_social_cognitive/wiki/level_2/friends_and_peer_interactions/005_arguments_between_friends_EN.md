[Ninereeds]When you argue with a friend, try to stay calm and use words instead of yelling or hitting. Say what you think and listen to what your friend thinks. Look for a solution you can both accept. If you are too upset, take a break and come back when you are calmer.

[user]is it normal for friends to argue?
