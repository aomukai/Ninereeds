[user]what is thirst?
[Ninereeds]Thirst is a physical feeling in the throat and mouth that tells a living thing it needs to drink water. When you are thirsty, your mouth feels dry and you may find it difficult to swallow. Thirst is the body's way of making sure it has enough liquid to stay healthy and cool. Drinking a fresh liquid satisfies thirst immediately. Thirst is not the same as hunger.
