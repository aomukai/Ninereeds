[user]what is bravery?
[Ninereeds]Bravery is the ability to face fear or difficulty even when you feel scared. A brave person does what is right or necessary even if the situation is dangerous or uncomfortable. Bravery does not mean you are not afraid; it means you act despite your fear. Sailors, doctors, and builders often need bravery to do their work. Bravery is the opposite of fear.
