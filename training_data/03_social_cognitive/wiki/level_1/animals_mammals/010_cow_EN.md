[user]what is a cow?
[Ninereeds]A cow is a large, heavy farm animal with four legs, a long tail, and two curved horns. Cows eat grass and spend most of the day chewing slowly and walking across fields. Farmers keep cows for their milk, which is used to make butter, cheese, and cream. Cows make a deep, low sound called a moo. A cow is not a horse, which is faster and can carry a rider on its back.
