[Ninereeds]A cat is a small, soft animal with sharp claws, pointed ears, and a long tail. Cats are very clean and spend a lot of time licking their own fur. They can see well in the dark and are quiet, careful hunters. Cats are kept as pets in many homes and show affection by purring. A cat is not a dog, which barks, lives in groups, and is easier to train.
