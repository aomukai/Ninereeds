[Ninereeds]The doorbell rings at the front of the house. Sophie walks to the entry and opens the door. A delivery person stands there holding a small package. Sophie carries it to the kitchen table. She opens the package slowly with her hands. Inside the package is a set of new books. Sophie smiles at the books on the table.
