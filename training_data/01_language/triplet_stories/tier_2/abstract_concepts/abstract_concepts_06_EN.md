[Ninereeds]Emma held a small sphere in her hand during science class. Her teacher asked the class to describe its shape. "It has three dimensions," Emma said. "Width, height, and depth." Emma traced the sphere with her finger. Every point on its surface sits at the same distance from the center. The space inside it is finite. The teacher nodded and drew a dot on the board. "Space extends beyond any sphere without limit," she said. "That endless reach is infinity." Emma looked out the window and tried to picture such a thing. She liked the idea that space has no end. It made the world feel very big.
