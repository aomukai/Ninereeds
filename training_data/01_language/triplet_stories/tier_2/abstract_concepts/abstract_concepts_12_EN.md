[Ninereeds]Owen watches his father bake bread each morning in the kitchen. "Why do you always make the same bread?" Owen asks. His father smiles and points to the bowl of flour. "This recipe came from my old teacher. The origin of this bread is in the ingredients and the steps." Owen watches his father mix the flour, water, and yeast. He sees the dough rise on the counter. After baking, the loaf comes out warm with a golden crust. His father stamps the date on a paper label. "The creation of each loaf follows the same process," his father says. He puts the bread on a shelf to cool. Owen understands now that the origin is in the baker's hands. The same hands that learned from a teacher and now share that knowledge with Owen.
