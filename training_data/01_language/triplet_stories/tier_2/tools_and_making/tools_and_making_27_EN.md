[Ninereeds]Sophie pulled her tennis racket from her bag and felt the loose strings. She knew that proper maintenance kept her equipment ready for every game. Sophie asked her dad, "Can you help me check the tension on these strings?" Her dad showed her how to run a finger across each string. A firm grip on the racket handle made all the difference during play. Sophie wiped the frame with a dry cloth to remove dirt from the court. She oiled the handle to keep the grip from wearing down. Her dad said, "That feels better, doesn't it?" Sophie nodded and tightened the last loose section. She placed the racket back in its bag with care. This routine was not hard to follow, and it kept the equipment safe for the next match.
