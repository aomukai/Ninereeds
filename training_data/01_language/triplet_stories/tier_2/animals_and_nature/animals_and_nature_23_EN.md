[Ninereeds]Ella walks through the clover field near the farm meadow. She sees a bee visiting each tight clover bud. The bee gathers pollen from the small white flowers. Ella watches the bee move from one bud to the next. "Why do you visit every clover bud?" she asks. The bee buzzes softly and keeps working. Ella kneels down and touches a soft clover leaf. She spots a bud that is not open yet. The bud holds a tiny drop of morning dew. Ella smiles and looks across the green field. The warm sun lights the meadow around her. Ella finds another clover with open petals. She whispers, "The bee will like this one." The clover field grows in the morning light.
