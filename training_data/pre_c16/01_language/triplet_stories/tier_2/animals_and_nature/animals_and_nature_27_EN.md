[Ninereeds]A long hiking trail crosses the open landscape between green hills and rocky ground. Max walks along the trail in the morning light. He carries a small bag and stops near an old stone trough under a tree branch. Cool water fills the trough. "This is a good place to rest," Max says. He sits on a flat rock beside the trough. A deer walks down from the hills and stops at the far end of the trough. Max stays very still. The deer watches him for a long moment. "You can drink," Max says softly. The deer bends its head and drinks from the still water. Birds call from the branches above the trough. The warm sun rests on the grass and rock around them.
