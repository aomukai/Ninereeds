[Ninereeds]Jack walked to the marsh near his home one morning. The water was muddy and the air smelled bad. Jack looked at his neighbor and asked, "Can we help this marsh?" She said, "A group started a conservation project last month. You can join them." Jack decided to volunteer with the group. They worked together to pull trash from the water. They cleared the polluted soil along the edge. Over the weeks, the water began to look cleaner. New plant growth appeared near the banks. A dragonfly landed on a reed beside the stream. Jack watched a fish swim through the clear water. The marsh was healing, and the ecosystem was coming back to life.
