[Ninereeds]Ella plants a flower seed in a small pot of soil. She waters the soil each morning before school. "How long until something happens?" she asks her mother. Her mother smiles and says to be patient and keep caring for it. After a week, a tiny green sprout pushes through the dark soil. Ella claps her hands and checks the pot every day. The stem grows taller and opens new leaves one by one. Each new leaf feels like a small improvement to Ella. She moves the plant to a larger pot for the roots. This gives the plant space for steady growth. By summer the flower stands tall with a bright yellow bloom. Development turns a small seed into a plant that towers over Ella. She learns that patience and daily care bring big changes.
