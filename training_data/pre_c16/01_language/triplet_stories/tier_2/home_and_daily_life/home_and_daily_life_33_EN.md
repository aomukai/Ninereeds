[Ninereeds]The family portrait hangs on the living room wall above the old cabinet. Luke steps into the room and looks at the photograph. He points at a small face in the wooden frame and asks, "Who is that boy?" His mother smiles and says, "That is your grandfather." Luke touches the glass over the portrait and thinks about all the years. His mother lifts him up for a closer look. "This item has been in our family for a long time," she tells him. Luke studies each person in the family display and feels proud. He asks if he can be in the next portrait. His mother hugs him and says that would make a wonderful picture. Every morning the light from the window falls on the portrait. This photograph is a treasure that holds the family together.
