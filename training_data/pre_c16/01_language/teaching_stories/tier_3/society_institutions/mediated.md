The boat had a stick in the middle, and a cloth tied to the stick. “Look,” the girl said. “The wind is pushing the boat.” Her mother smiled. “The wind does not touch the boat directly,” she said. “The cloth catches the wind, and then the cloth moves the boat. She showed it to her father.
