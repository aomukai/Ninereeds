Riku pushed his bicycle. The bag on the front was full. It pulled the handlebars to one side. He pushed harder.

Stefan came down the street. His hands were behind his back. He walked slow.

Riku stopped. Stefan stopped.

"Full bag," Stefan said.

Riku nodded. "Heavy today."

Stefan looked at the bag. Then he looked at the bicycle. Then he nodded back. He walked on.

Riku pushed the bicycle to the post box. The red one set into the wall. He leaned the bicycle against the gate. The gate creaked.

He opened the bag. Letters inside. Thick ones and thin ones. He took out a handful. The paper was warm from being carried.

He pulled the post box handle. The door opened. Dark inside. He pushed the letters in. They fell with a soft sound. Down into the box.

He took out another handful. Then another. The bag got lighter.

He closed the post box. The handle clicked.

He swung his leg over the bicycle. He sat on the seat. The bag was light now. It did not pull. He pushed the pedal. The wheels turned. He rode away down the street.
