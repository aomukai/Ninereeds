The wind was strong at the top of the hill.

Emma leaned forward. The wind pushed against her coat. It flattened her hair against her head. She had to lean just to stand still.

Taro's hat lifted off his head.

It flew. It tumbled through the air. It hit the grass and rolled down the slope.

"Hey!" Taro shouted.

Biscuit was already running. He chased the hat down the hill. The hat rolled. Biscuit pounced. The hat skidded away. He pounced again.

Emma turned her face into the wind. It howled in her ears. There were no trees here. No shelter. Just the long slope and the sky and the wind coming from somewhere far away.

"Lean into it," she said.

Taro leaned. The wind held him. He did not fall.

Biscuit caught the hat. He trotted back up the slope with it in his mouth. The hat was dirty. Taro took it and held it tight.

The wind blew. The grass bent. The sky was clear and cold.

They stood at the top and let the wind push against them.
