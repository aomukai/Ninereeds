The door opened. Cold air came in fast.

Stefan stepped inside. He closed the door behind him. The bell rang once.

The bakery was warm. The window was steamed over. Hana stood behind the counter with flour on her apron.

Stefan said nothing. He just stood there, letting the cold leave his coat.

Hana reached under the counter. She brought out a paper bag. The bottom was warm. She handed it across.

Stefan took it. He held it in both hands for a moment. Then he put one hand in his pocket and touched a coin.

He held it out.

Hana shook her head.

Stefan looked at her. She did not say anything. He put the coin back.

He nodded once.

Then he turned and pushed the door open. The bell rang again. Cold air rushed in — sharp, sudden, full of the street.

He stepped out.

The door closed.

The bell was quiet.

The room was warm again. Hana wiped her hands on her apron and turned back to the counter.
