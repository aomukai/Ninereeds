The snow lay thick over Gran’s field. Emma’s boots sank in past her ankles. Taro dragged the sled behind him, his breath puffing white in the cold.  

"First one down!" he yelled, flopping onto the sled. Biscuit barked and raced alongside as Taro pushed off. The sled flew fast down the hill, spraying snow. Biscuit skidded at the bottom, his paws sliding.  

Emma packed a handful of snow tight. She threw it at Taro as he trudged back up. It hit his shoulder and burst into flakes. His cheeks were red, his mittens clumped with snow.  

"Your turn," he panted.  

Emma sat on the sled. The cold seeped through her trousers. She pushed off with her hands. The wind stung her face. Halfway down, the sled hit a bump. She tipped sideways and tumbled into the snow. Biscuit licked her ear.  

Gran called from the oak. "Hot cocoa’s ready!"  

Emma’s mittens dripped as she stood. The sled left a dark trail in the snow all the way back up the hill.
