The fog was thick on the meadow path. Emma stood at the gate and looked. She could not see the pond. She could not see the oak.

Taro walked ahead. His shape went grey, then faint, then gone.

"Taro?"

His voice came back. "I am still here."

Biscuit pushed through the grass beside the path. The grass was wet. The fog beaded on Emma's coat. She wiped her cheek with her sleeve.

She took a step. The path was there under her feet. Another step. Still there.

Then a sound came through the fog. A bell. One note, then another. It was the school bell.

Emma stopped. "That is close."

Taro's voice came again, closer now. "It sounds close."

The bell rang a third time. Biscuit sneezed somewhere in the grass.

Emma took another step. The fog moved around her. She could not see Taro, but she could hear him. She could hear his feet on the path.

"Keep walking," she said.

His feet kept walking. The path was there. The bell was there. She followed the sound.
