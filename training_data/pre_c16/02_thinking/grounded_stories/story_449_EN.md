Biscuit went ahead on the meadow path. His nose was low to the ground. He did not look back.

Bello stayed behind. He stopped. He turned his head.

Dou sat on a fence post near the garden gate. Her tail was very straight. One white ear pointed at Bello. She did not move.

Bello took a step toward her. Then another. The grass was wet around his paws. Dou watched him. Her eyes were yellow and still.

Bello stopped. He looked at Dou. He looked at the path ahead, where Biscuit was getting smaller. He took one more step toward the fence post. Then he stopped again.

Dou blinked. Once. Slowly.

Bello turned. He ran after Biscuit. His paws hit the path in quick, soft thumps. He did not look back.

The fence post was empty. The grass where Bello had stood was bent. Dou sat on the post, tail straight, watching the path where the dogs had gone.
