Stefan walked up the hill with his hands behind his back. His bicycle was not with him today. He walked his beat slowly, even on a hill.

Emma and Taro were halfway up with Bello. Bello ran in circles around Stefan's legs, then ran ahead, then came back.

"You are going to the top?" Emma asked.

"I am," Stefan said. "You can see everything from up there."

They climbed together. The grass was short and stiff with frost. The wind hit them before they reached the top.

At the top, Stefan stopped. He looked down at the village below.

"You can see the mill chimney," he said. He pointed. "And the lane. And every roof between."

Emma looked. The roofs were grey and brown. Some had smoke. The mill chimney rose straight up, thinner than she expected from this far away.

Taro pointed at something. "That is Gran's chimney."

"It is," Stefan said.

The wind was cold. It came across the hilltop without anything to stop it. Bello pressed close to Emma's legs.

Stefan did not move. He stood and looked at the village as if he had time for it. Then he turned and started back down.

Emma and Taro followed. Bello ran ahead again.
