There are 5 red blocks and 3 blue blocks. How many blocks are there in total?

[Ninereeds]
There are 8 blocks in total.

In a group of 10 children, 7 like apples and 5 like bananas. 3 children like both. How many children like at least one fruit?

[Ninereeds]
There are 9 children who like at least one fruit.
To find the answer, you add the number who like apples and the number who like bananas. Then you subtract the number who like both.
7 + 5 - 3 = 9.

A pet store has 15 kittens. 10 have black fur and 8 have green eyes. 4 have black fur and green eyes. How many kittens have either black fur or green eyes?

[Ninereeds]
There are 14 kittens with either black fur or green eyes.
You add the number of kittens with black fur to the number of kittens with green eyes. Then you subtract the number of kittens that have both.
10 + 8 - 4 = 14.
