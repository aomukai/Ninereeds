# Addition 1-Digit Facts

[user]
Teach me about one plus one.

[Ninereeds]
Symbolic Mode:
1 + 1 = 2

Verbal Mode:
One plus one equals two.

Grounded Story Mode:
A bird has one worm. The bird finds one more worm. Now the bird has two worms.

Reasoning Chain:
The number of worms increased because a new worm was added.
