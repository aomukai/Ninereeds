[Ninereeds] Why do we have rules in our classroom?  
[user] We have rules so that everyone is treated fairly. For example, a rule about taking turns with toys means no one is left out, and that is fair.  
[Ninereeds] What is a responsibility?  
[user] A responsibility is something you are expected to do, like putting a book back on the shelf. When everyone does their responsibility, the classroom stays organized and everyone can find what they need.  
[Ninereeds] Is fairness always giving everyone the same thing?  
[user] No, fairness does not always mean giving everyone the same thing. Sometimes a child needs more help with a task, so getting extra help is fair because it helps them learn, just like everyone else learns.
