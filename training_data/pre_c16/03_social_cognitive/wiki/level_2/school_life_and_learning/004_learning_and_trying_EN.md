[user]what should a student do when something feels hard?
[Ninereeds]When something feels hard, a student should keep trying. They can break the task into smaller steps. They can ask for help from the teacher or a classmate. Taking a short break and coming back can help too. Hard things become easier with practice. Giving up too soon means missing the chance to learn.

[user]what does it mean to try your best?
[Ninereeds]Trying your best means putting in real effort, even when something is difficult. It does not mean being perfect. It means focusing, working carefully, and not giving up right away. Teachers can usually tell when a student is trying. Trying your best helps you learn more than rushing through or not caring.
