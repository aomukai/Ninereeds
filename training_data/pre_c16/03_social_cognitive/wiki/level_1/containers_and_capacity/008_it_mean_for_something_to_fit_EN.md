[user]what does it mean for something to fit?
[Ninereeds]To fit means to go into a space in the right way or to be the right size for it. A toy may fit in a box, or a book may fit in a bag. If something does not fit, the space may be too small or the thing may be too big. Fit helps people choose the right container or place. To fit is not the same as to spill out.
