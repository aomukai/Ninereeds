[Ninereeds]Jealousy is the feeling of wanting what someone else has or wishing their good thing were yours. A person may feel jealousy when another child gets praise, a prize, or a toy. Jealousy can make it hard to feel happy for someone else. People often need honesty and calm words to handle jealousy well. Jealousy is not the same as gratitude.
