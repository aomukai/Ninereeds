What is announcing?

[Ninereeds]

She looked around. No one saw her. She stepped onto a low bench. Her voice rose above the others.

“I have the best husk!” she called.

A boy stopped. Two women stopped. The farmer put down his basket.

The old lady waved her hand. “Zoom over here,” she said. “One bag subtracts two coins from the price.”

Her hands waved in the air. Her voice was clear like a queen’s. The people gathered close.

She was announcing. The bench and the voice and the hands — all of it made the news go far.

Later, a child walked to the park and tried the same thing. He stepped onto a stone. “Look at me!” he shouted.

The dog looked. Then the dog ran off. The child laughed. He was learning to announce, too.
