"It rained last night," she said. On the ground, many tracks crossed the mud. "Many animals came here," she whispered. An empty bowl sat on the doorstep of a cottage. "I was just guessing," she said. The old woman gave her a shoelace to tie. "Don't whine about your mistake. A good guess helps, but you must look closer. Never assign a cause too fast.
