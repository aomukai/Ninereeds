[Ninereeds]A subscription can be renewed. A library card can be renewed. A driver's license can be renewed. Energy can be renewed by resting. Old furniture can be renewed by fixing it. A commitment can be renewed by promising again. A friendship can be renewed by reconnecting. Trust can be renewed by showing honesty again. A lease or contract can be renewed when it ends. A forest can be renewed after a fire by new growth. A passport can be renewed when it expires. A thread or conversation can be renewed by picking it up again. A sense of purpose or hope can be renewed. A ritual or tradition can be renewed by continuing it. A system or structure can be renewed by repairing and updating it. Many things that age, expire, fade, or weaken can be renewed. Not everything can be renewed. A broken bone can heal but cannot literally be renewed. A lost moment cannot be renewed. A dead animal cannot be renewed. Something completely destroyed cannot be renewed. Something that never existed cannot be renewed. Renewing applies to things that can return to a previous good state. It requires possibility. It requires something to work with. Renewing is not creating from nothing. It is restoring or continuing what was already there.
