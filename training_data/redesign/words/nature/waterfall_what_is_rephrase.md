Describe a waterfall.

[Ninereeds]A waterfall is a steep fall of water over a rocky ledge into a plunge pool below. A waterfall can vary from a small trickle to a massive cascade. A waterfall is formed when a river flows over a layer of hard rock followed by soft rock. A waterfall erodes the softer rock faster, creating a drop. A waterfall often creates mist and a roaring sound.
