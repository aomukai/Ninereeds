The shed smelled of wood and old paint. Taro held the plank steady while Gran marked it with a pencil. "Saw here," she said, and went back to sorting nails.  

The saw stuck halfway. Taro pushed harder. The plank jerked.  

"Hold still," Gran said. She put her hand on the far end. The saw moved smooth after that.  

Taro lined up two planks and banged a nail in. The shelf wobbled. He banged another nail. It wobbled more.  

Gran squinted at it. "Crooked," she said. She tapped the left side. "Short leg."  

Taro measured again. The right leg was longer. He sawed a bit off. The nails went in straight this time.  

"Try again," Gran said, handing him the hammer.  

He missed. The hammer hit his thumb. He shook his hand and tried again. The nail went in.  

The shelf stood steady. Gran nodded and went back to her nails. Taro ran his finger along the edge. Smooth.
