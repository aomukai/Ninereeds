The bench under the willow was warm from the afternoon sun. Gran sat with her hands in her lap, watching the pond. Emma knelt on the flat stone at the water’s edge.  

The water was still. It reflected the sky like a mirror. Below the surface, small dark shapes drifted near the bottom. Fish. They moved slowly, barely stirring the silt. Emma held her breath.  

She shifted her weight. The stone wobbled. Her shadow fell across the water.  

The fish vanished.  

Emma looked up. "Gran—"  

Gran smiled but didn’t turn. "Hmm?"  

Emma stared at the water. The reflection of the willow trembled where the fish had been. She leaned back carefully. The shadow lifted.  

A moment passed. Then another.  

One fish reappeared. Then two. They hovered near the bottom as if nothing had happened.  

Emma tucked her knees under her and didn’t move. The pond stayed quiet. The fish stayed there.  

Gran brushed a leaf from her sleeve. The willow rustled.
