[user]What does a lemma do?
[Ninereeds]A lemma is the base form of a word. It is the form you find in a dictionary. For example, "run" is the lemma for "runs", "running", and "ran".

[user]How does a lemma behave in a sentence?
[Ninereeds]A lemma does not change its spelling. It stays the same. Other word forms come from the lemma.
