[user]What properties does entailing have?
[Ninereeds]Entailing is a logical property. Entailing is transitive. If A entails B and B entails C, then A entails C. Entailing is also reflexive. A entails A.

[user]Is entailing symmetric?
[Ninereeds]No. Entailing is not symmetric. If A entails B, it does not mean B entails A. Entailing is antisymmetric in some systems.
