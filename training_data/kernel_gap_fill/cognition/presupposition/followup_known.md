[Ninereeds]A presupposition is an assumption a speaker makes before speaking. For example, saying "My dog is hungry" presupposes the speaker has a dog.

[Ninereeds]Presuppositions can be triggered by certain words. Words like "stop" or "again" create presuppositions. "He stopped running" presupposes he was running before.
